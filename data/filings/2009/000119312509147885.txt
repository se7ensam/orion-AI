 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="right"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center">

<IMG SRC="g29207img001.jpg" ALT="LOGO"> </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>AEGON reaches final agreement to reduce charges on unit-linked insurance policies in the Netherlands </B></FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">AEGON has reached a final agreement with <I>Stichting Verliespolis</I> and <I>Stichting Woekerpolis Claim</I> to reduce charges for customers of its unit-linked insurance
policies in the Netherlands. The agreement is part of industry-wide efforts in the Netherlands to address concerns over charges related to unit-linked insurance products. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">AEGON previously announced improvements to such policies last September. Based on the information available at that time, the company estimated the cost at approximately EUR 140&nbsp;million before tax. Under the
final agreement announced today, AEGON will commit an additional maximum amount of EUR 110&nbsp;million before tax to improve terms and conditions of its unit-linked policies, which includes a maximum of EUR 80&nbsp;million before tax to assist
those customers experiencing adverse financial circumstances. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">As a result of the final agreement, there will be an additional impact on the company&#146;s
embedded value of approximately EUR 70&nbsp;million after tax. The cost of improvements will be reflected in AEGON&#146;s earnings over the remainder of the lifespan of affected policies. Consequently, AEGON expects no significant impact on its 2009
income statement as it has adequate provisions. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The final agreement is the result of several months of negotiations between AEGON and organizations in the
Netherlands representing the interests of customers. These organizations will be involved in supervising the implementation of the agreement. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We are
pleased to have reached a good agreement for our customers&#148;, said Marco Keim, member of AEGON&#146;s Management Board. &#147;The Dutch Financial Service Ombudsman has examined the AEGON agreement and confirmed that it is fully in line with his
Recommendation and consistent with prior agreements of other insurance companies&#148;. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Before this agreement, AEGON had made substantial improvements by
reducing costs and premiums for its unit-linked insurance products in the Netherlands. These previous improvements, introduced over a number of years, are valued at approximately EUR 380&nbsp;million before tax. AEGON&#146;s unit-linked portfolio
consists of 1.3&nbsp;million policies, of which 50% have a guaranteed minimum return. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="font-size:12px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px">&nbsp;</P> <P
STYLE="line-height:1px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:2px;border-bottom:1pt solid #000000">&nbsp;</P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="right"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">20090713 </FONT></P>
