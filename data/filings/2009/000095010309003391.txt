closing are the transfer of the NEW HBU II shares to the new ABN AMRO Bank N.V.
and the legal demerger of the majority of the businesses acquired by the Dutch
State into the new ABN AMRO Bank N.V. The closing of the NEW HBU II and IFN
Finance transaction<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-STYLE: italic">&#160;</font>is expected to take
place thereafter in the early spring of 2010.</font></div>

analysis, the transaction will have a negative impact of between EUR 800 and EUR
900 million. The total loss on the transaction includes the impact of a
