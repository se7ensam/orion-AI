<P ALIGN=center><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE="3"><B>Aladdin
Knowledge Systems Enters Into Merger Agreement with <BR>Vector Capital Affiliate</B> </FONT> </P>


<P align=center><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE="2"><I> Aladdin Shareholders to Receive $11.50 per Share in Cash<BR>
Transaction Valued at $160 Million</I> </FONT>
</P>


<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B>TEL AVIV, ISRAEL AND SAN
FRANSCISO, CA &#150; January 12, 2009</B> &#150; Aladdin Knowledge Systems Ltd. (NASDAQ:
ALDN) today announced that it has entered into a definitive merger agreement to be
acquired by an investor group lead by Vector Capital, a leading private equity firm
specializing in the technology industry, in a transaction valued at approximately $160
million. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Under the terms of the agreement,
Aladdin shareholders will receive $11.50 per share in cash for each share of common stock
they hold. This represents a premium of approximately 20% over Aladdin&#146;s closing
share price on January 9, 2009, the last trading day prior to today&#146;s announcement,
and a premium of approximately 64% over Aladdin&#146;s closing share price on January 5,
2009, the last trading day prior to Aladdin&#146;s announcement that it was in continuing
discussions with Vector Capital regarding a possible strategic transaction. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&#147;After extensive negotiations
and careful and thorough analysis, conducted with our independent advisors, the Board has
unanimously endorsed this transaction as in the best interests of the Company and our
shareholders,&#148; said Yanki Margalit, Chairman and CEO of Aladdin Knowledge Systems. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Commenting on the announcement, David
Fishman, Partner, Vector Capital, said, &#147;We are very excited about adding Aladdin to
our portfolio of outstanding technology companies and believe that placing Aladdin&#146;s
DRM and Authentication assets under common management with those of our portfolio company,
SafeNet Inc., a global leader in information security, makes considerable strategic sense
and will greatly benefit all stakeholders. Aladdin is an exceptional company, with
significant potential and a strong commitment to its employees, partners and customers
worldwide. We understand Aladdin&#146;s industry and business well, and look forward to
working collaboratively to ensure a smooth and expeditious transition.&#148; </FONT></P>

<p align=center>
<font size=2></font></p>
<HR SIZE="1" NOSHADE  STYLE="margin-top: -2px">
<HR SIZE="4" NOSHADE  STYLE="margin-top: -10px">

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>The transaction is subject to certain
closing conditions, including the approval of Aladdin&#146;s shareholders, antitrust
regulatory approvals, and the satisfaction of other customary closing conditions.
Approximately 14% of Aladdin&#146;s outstanding shares in the aggregate are held by an
affiliate of Vector Capital, and Directors of Aladdin, including Mr. Margalit, have
entered into a voting agreement&nbsp;for 19% of the outstanding shares pursuant to which
they have&nbsp;agreed to vote all of&nbsp;their shares in favor of the transaction. There
is no financing condition to consummate the transaction. The transaction is expected to
close following the satisfaction of all closing conditions, which is anticipated to occur
in the next two to three months. Wells Fargo Foothill, part of Wells Fargo &amp; Company
(NYSE:WFC), has committed to provide debt financing in support of the acquisition. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>The transaction will be presented to
Aladdin shareholders for approval at Aladdin&#146;s Extraordinary General Meeting, which
will be scheduled as soon as practicable. </FONT></P>

<p ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE="2"><B>About Aladdin</B> <BR>Aladdin Knowledge Systems (NASDAQ:
ALDN) is an information security leader with offices in 15 countries, a worldwide network
of channel partners, and numerous awards for innovation. Aladdin eToken is the
world&#146;s #1 USB-based authentication solution, offering identity and access management
tools that protect sensitive data. Aladdin SafeWord two-factor authentication technology
protects companies&#146; important information assets and applications. Aladdin HASP SRM
boosts growth for software developers and publishers through strong anti-piracy
protection, IP protection, and secure licensing and product activation. Aladdin eSafe
delivers real-time intelligent Web gateway security that helps protect data and networks,
improves productivity, and enables compliance. Visit <U>www.Aladdin.com</U>. </FONT></P>
