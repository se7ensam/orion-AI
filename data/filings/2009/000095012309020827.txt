<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 12pt"><B>Abbey National plc &#151; Pro Forma Financial Information</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt"><B>Unaudited Pro Forma Financial Information</B>

</DIV>
<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt"><B>Abbey and Alliance &#038; Leicester unaudited pro forma condensed consolidated financial information</B>
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">On 9 January&nbsp;2009, in order to optimise the capital, liquidity funding and overall financial
efficiency of the Santander group, Banco Santander, S.A. transferred all of its Alliance &#038;
Leicester plc shares to Abbey National plc in exchange for 12,631,375,230 Abbey National plc newly
issued ordinary shares (the &#145;Transfer&#146;). The result of this was to increase Abbey National plc&#146;s
holding of 35.6% of Alliance &#038; Leicester plc&#146;s equity voting interests to 100%. Accordingly, Abbey
National plc is now the immediate parent company of Alliance &#038; Leicester plc.
</DIV>



<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The following unaudited pro forma condensed consolidated income statement, balance sheet and
explanatory notes (&#145;the unaudited pro forma financial information&#146;) illustrate the impact of the
Transfer on Abbey National plc&#146;s historical financial position and results of operation on the
basis set out in Note 1. It is important that the pro forma balance sheet and pro forma income
statement are read together with the explanatory notes and the consolidated financial statements
for the year ended 31 December&nbsp;2008 of Abbey and Alliance &#038; Leicester as filed with the US
Securities and Exchange Commission in the Form 20-F dated 19 March 2009 and Form 6-K dated 8 July&nbsp;2009, respectively.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The pro forma financial information is presented for illustrative purposes only and, because
of its nature, it may not give a true picture of what the operating results or the financial
position of the enlarged group would have been had the Transfer occurred on the respective dates
assumed. Also, because of its nature, it may not give a true picture of future operating results or
the financial position of the enlarged group.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The final allocation of the purchase price will be determined after completion of a thorough
analysis to determine the fair values of Alliance &#038; Leicester&#146;s assets and liabilities as at the
date set out in Note 2 (the &#145;effective date&#146;). Accordingly, the final accounting adjustments and
goodwill may be materially different from those reflected in the pro forma financial information
presented in this document.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The pro forma financial information does not constitute statutory accounts as defined in
Section&nbsp;435 of the UK Companies Act nor under any other rule; and has not been reviewed by any
auditor or independent accountant.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 12pt"><B>Note 2 &#151; Acquisition</B>
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">On 9 January&nbsp;2009, in order to optimise the capital, liquidity funding and overall financial
efficiency of the Santander group, Banco Santander, S.A. transferred all of its Alliance &#038;
Leicester plc (wholly owned by Santander and Abbey) shares to Abbey National plc in exchange for
12,631,375,230 Abbey National plc newly issued ordinary shares (the &#145;Transfer&#146;). The result of this
was to increase Abbey National plc&#146;s holding of 35.6% of Alliance &#038; Leicester plc&#146;s equity voting
interests to 100%. Accordingly, Abbey National plc is now the immediate parent company of Alliance
&#038; Leicester plc. IFRS 3 &#145;Business Combination&#146; excludes from its scope the accounting for
transactions between entities under common control. In the absence of authoritative guidance under
IFRS for accounting for such transactions, the transfer will be accounted for in a manner
consistent with group reconstruction relief under UK GAAP, as allowed by IAS 8 paragraph 10. Under this guidance, the transfer of
Alliance &#038; Leicester plc will be accounted for by Abbey National plc with effect from 10 October
2008 (the &#145;effective date&#146;), the date on which Banco Santander, S.A. acquired control of Alliance &#038;
Leicester plc. The ordinary shares of Abbey National plc issued as consideration for Banco
Santander, S.A.&#146;s holding of Alliance &#038; Leicester plc shares will be recognised at their nominal
value which is the same as the fair value of the shares issued by Banco Santander, S.A. in exchange for the shares of Alliance &#038; Leicester plc plus acquisition costs, and the net assets of Alliance &#038; Leicester plc will be accounted for by Abbey at the fair
values recognised by Banco Santander, S.A. at the time of its acquisition of Alliance &#038; Leicester
plc on 10 October&nbsp;2008. Under the group reconstruction relief accounting, Abbey&#146;s financial
statements for the year ended 31 December&nbsp;2008 will be recast to reflect this accounting treatment.
