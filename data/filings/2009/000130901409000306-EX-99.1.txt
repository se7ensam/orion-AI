Ballard Power Systems Announces Annual General Meeting


For Immediate Release – May 4, 2009


VANCOUVER, CANADA — Ballard Power Systems (TSX: BLD; NASDAQ: BLDP) will hold its Annual General
Meeting on Tuesday, June 2, 2009, at 1:00pm PST at the Pan Pacific Hotel, 999 Canada Place,
Vancouver, BC.


Included in the event, Mr. John Sheridan, Ballard Power Systems’ President and Chief Executive
Officer, will discuss the company’s strategy and progress.


This event will be audio webcast and will be accessible on Ballard’s web site at www.ballard.com.
The webcast will be archived for replay for one month.


About Ballard Power Systems


Ballard Power Systems (TSX: BLD; NASDAQ: BLDP) is recognized as a world leader in the design,
development, manufacture and sale of clean energy fuel cell products. Ballard’s mission is to
accelerate fuel cell product adoption. To learn more about what Ballard is doing with Power to
Change the World®, visit www.ballard.com.
