    <p style="text-align: left">
      AIXTRON AG / Release of&#160;&#160;announcements according to Article 21, Section
      1, 24 of the WpHG [the German Securities Trading Act] (share)
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      26.05.2009
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      On May 22, 2009, Oppenheimer Funds Inc., Centennial, CO, USA has
      informed us according to Article 21, Section 1, 24 of the WpHG that via
      shares its Voting Rights on AIXTRON AG, Aachen, Deutschland, ISIN:
      DE000A0WMPJ6, WKN: A0WMPJ, have fallen below the 5% limit of the Voting
      Rights on May 15, 2009 and now amount to 4.95% (this corresponds to
      4499969 Voting Rights).
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      According to Article 22, Section 1, Sentence 1, No. 6 of the WpHG, 4.95%
      of the Voting Rights (this corresponds to 4499969 Voting Rights) were to
      be attributed to the company. 4.95% of those (4499969 voting rights) are
      to be attributed via Oppenheimer Global Opportunities Fund, Centennial,
      CO, USA.
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      On May 22, 2009, Oppenheimer Acquisition Corp., Centennial, CO, USA has
      informed us according to Article 21, Section 1, 24 of the WpHG that via
      shares its Voting Rights on AIXTRON AG, Aachen, Deutschland, ISIN:
      DE000A0WMPJ6, WKN: A0WMPJ, have fallen below the 5% limit of the Voting
      Rights on May 15, 2009 and now amount to 4.95% (this corresponds to
      4499969 Voting Rights).
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      According to Article 22, Section 1, Sentence 1, No. 6, Sentence 2 of the
      WpHG, 4.95% of the Voting Rights (this corresponds to 4499969 Voting
      Rights) were to be attributed to the company. 4.95% of those (4499969
      voting rights) are to be attributed via Oppenheimer Global Opportunities
      Fund, Centennial, CO, USA.
    </p>
