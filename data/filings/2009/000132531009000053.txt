<P style="line-height:normal; margin:0px; padding-bottom:4px; font-size:14pt; border-bottom:1px solid #000000" align=center><B>ALDA Enters Into Supplier Agreement With</B><FONT style="font-size:12pt"><B> </FONT>T&amp;T Supermarket</B></P>
<P style="margin:0px"><BR></P>
<P style="line-height:14pt; margin:0px; font-size:12pt">Vancouver, BC - ALDA Pharmaceuticals Corp. (APH:TSX-V, OTCBB:APCSF) (&#147;the Company&#148; or &#147;ALDA&#148;) announces that it has entered into a Supplier Agreement with T&amp;T Supermarket Inc. (&#147;T&amp;T&#148;). ALDA&#146;s products will be distributed through T&amp;T&#146;s 17 stores in BC, Alberta and Ontario. Dr Terrance Owen, President &amp; CEO states, &#147;This agreement with T&amp;T is an important step in achieving distribution of our products to consumers. We look forward to forging other partnerships that are equally as important as this one.&#148;</P>
<P style="margin:0px"><BR></P>
<P style="line-height:14pt; margin:0px; font-size:12pt"><B>About T&amp;T Supermarket Inc. (www.tnt-supermarket.com)</B></P>
<P style="margin:0px"><BR></P>
<P style="line-height:14pt; margin:0px; font-size:12pt">T&amp;T is Canada's largest Asian supermarket chain with eight stores in Greater Vancouver, two in Calgary, two in Edmonton and five stores in Greater Toronto, making a total of 17 stores in the chain. T&amp;T features many uniquely Asian food products that are not available in other retail outlets with the goal of enriching the lifestyle of Asian families in Canada and introducing the Asian food culture to Canada&#146;s multicultural society.</P>
<P style="margin:0px"><BR></P>
<P style="line-height:14pt; margin:0px; font-size:12pt"><B>About ALDA Pharmaceuticals Corp.</B></P>
<P style="margin:0px"><BR></P>
<P style="line-height:14pt; margin:0px; font-size:12pt">ALDA is focused on the development of infection-control therapeutics derived from its patented T<SUP>3</SUP>6<SUP>&#174;</SUP> technology. The company trades on the TSX Venture Exchange under the symbol APH and on the OTCBB under the symbol APCSF.</P>
