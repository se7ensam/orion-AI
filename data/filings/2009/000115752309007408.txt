    <p style="text-align: center">
      AIXTRON Aktiengesellschaft generates gross proceeds of approx. EUR 159.4
      million from capital increase
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      Aachen, October 29, 2009 - AIXTRON Aktiengesellschaft (ISIN
      DE000A0WMPJ6), is increasing its share capital by approximately 9.8 per
      cent through use of authorized capital and excluding shareholders'
      subscription rights.
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      The 8,979,937 new registered shares (shares without par value) were
      subscribed by investors via an accelerated bookbuilding process, the
      placement has been oversubscribed. The placement price was EUR 17.75 per
      share. The gross proceeds for the Company from the capital increase
      amount to approximately EUR 159.4 million. The proceeds from the issue
      will be used to strengthen the Company's balance sheet, to support
      higher capital spending and further growth of the Company.
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      The new shares will be admitted for trading without a sales prospectus
      in the regulated market of the Frankfurt Stock Exchange with a
      simultaneous listing in the Prime Standard segment of the Frankfurt
      Stock Exchange, which has enhanced disclosure obligations. The shares
      qualify for a dividend from January 1, 2009. Due to the necessary
      technical steps to consummate the described transaction, the delivery of
      the new shares and settlement is expected to occur on Tuesday, November
      3, 2009.
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      Joint Lead Managers and Joint Book Runners for the transaction are
      Deutsche Bank Aktiengesellschaft and J. P. Morgan Securities Ltd.
