    Notice
Concerning the Determination of the Subscription Price and Other Terms of Stock
Options (Stock Acquisition Rights) to be Issued by Advantest to its
Employees
    
    Tokyo
–April 1, 2009 – Advantest Corporation (the “Company”) resolved at a meeting of
its Board of Directors on March 24, 2009 to issue stock options in the form of
stock acquisition rights pursuant to a shareholders resolution at the 66th
ordinary general meeting of shareholders on June 25, 2008.  The
specific terms and conditions of the issuance of stock options in the form of
stock acquisition rights that were not determined at the meeting of its Board of
Directors on March 24, 2009 have been determined as set forth
below.
