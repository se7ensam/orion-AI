Ballard Announces 2009 First Quarter Financial Results Conference Call


For Immediate Release – April 8, 2009


VANCOUVER, CANADA — Ballard Power Systems (TSX: BLD; NASDAQ: BLDP) will hold a conference call on
Wednesday, April 29, 2009 at 8:00 a.m. PST (11:00 a.m. EST). John Sheridan, President and CEO, and
Bruce Cousins, Chief Financial Officer, will present Ballard’s 2009 first quarter financial
results.


The live call can be accessed by calling +1-604-638-5340. The live audio webcast can be accessed
through a link on Ballard’s homepage (www.ballard.com).


Following the call, an audio recording will be available for approximately 24 hours and can be
accessed at +1-604-638-9010 using confirmation number 6325#. The audio webcast with slides will
also be archived in the Investor Events & Conference Calls section of Ballard’s website.


About Ballard Power Systems

Ballard Power Systems (TSX: BLD; NASDAQ: BLDP) is recognized as a world leader in the design,
development, manufacture and sale of clean energy fuel cell products. Ballard’s mission is to
accelerate fuel cell product adoption. To learn more about what Ballard is doing with Power to
Change the World®, visit www.ballard.com.
