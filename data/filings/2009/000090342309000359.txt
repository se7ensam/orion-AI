<div align="center"><b><font face="Arial" size=2>ABBEY AND ALLIANCE &amp; LEICESTER MANAGEMENT CHANGES</font></b></div>
  <p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>Abbey National plc (&#147;Abbey&#148;) and Alliance &amp; Leicester plc (&#147;A&amp;L&#148;) have announced that David Bennett, Executive Director, will be leaving the Company.</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>David currently has Executive responsibility for the Intermediaries business and fulfilled the role of Integration Advisor following Santander&#146;s acquisition of A&amp;L in October 2008. He will remain with the business until 31 July 2009 in order to ensure a smooth handover of responsibility for Intermediaries which will be assumed by another member of Abbey&#146;s Executive Management Team.</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>Ant&#243;nio Horta-Os&#243;rio, Chief Executive, said: &#147;I would like to thank David for his help in the significant progress we&#146;ve made in integrating A&amp;L into the Santander Group. We wish him well for the future.&#148;</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>
