</TABLE> <P STYLE="line-height:1px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:2px;border-bottom:1pt solid #000000">&nbsp;</P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="5"><B>Agrium
commences exchange offer to acquire CF Industries </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>March&nbsp;16, 2009 - ALL AMOUNTS ARE STATED IN U.S.$ </B></FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">CALGARY, Alberta &#150; Agrium Inc. (TSX and NYSE: AGU) today announced that it has commenced an exchange offer for all of the outstanding shares of CF Industries
Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: CF) (the &#147;Offer&#148;). Under the terms of the Offer, CF stockholders would receive $31.70 in cash and 1.0 common share of Agrium for each CF share. Alternatively, CF stockholders have the option of electing to receive for
each CF share either 1.7866 common shares of Agrium or $72.00 in cash, subject to proration. In the aggregate, not more than 44% of the shares tendered will be exchanged for cash and not more than 56% of the shares tendered will be exchanged for
Agrium common shares. The Offer and withdrawal rights will expire at 12:00 midnight New York City time on May&nbsp;19, 2009, unless extended. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman"
SIZE="2">&#147;CF&#146;s refusal to engage in discussions with Agrium left us with no choice but to take our offer directly to CF stockholders. We believe CF&#146;s restructuring of its offer for Terra to circumvent a vote by its own stockholders
highlights their belief that CF would lose a stockholder vote and demonstrates a disregard for the rights of their stockholders,&#148; said Agrium President and CEO Mike Wilson. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We&nbsp;believe the Agrium offer is a far superior alternative for CF stockholders as&nbsp;they receive a premium rather than pay a premium to Terra stockholders.&nbsp;We are committed to this compelling
combination and urge CF stockholders to send a message to the CF Board by tendering their shares into the Agrium Offer.&nbsp;We would be&nbsp;prepared to increase our offer to reflect additional value that the CF Board and management can demonstrate
arising from the combination of our two companies.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The Offer is not subject to a financing condition. Agrium has sufficient cash resources and
committed financing underwritten by Royal Bank of Canada and The Bank of Nova Scotia to fund the cash portion of the Offer. The Offer is subject to certain conditions described in the offer to exchange. </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">RBC Capital Markets is acting as dealer manager; Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton&nbsp;&amp; Garrison LLP and Blake, Cassels&nbsp;&amp; Graydon LLP as legal counsel; and
Georgeson Inc. as information agent in connection with the Offer. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Stockholder questions regarding the exchange offer or requests for offering materials
should be directed to Agrium&#146;s information agent for the exchange offer, Georgeson Inc., toll-free at (866)&nbsp;318-0506. Offering materials are also available on the SEC&#146;s web site at www.sec.gov. CF stockholders are urged to read the
offering materials filed by Agrium, which contain important information about the Offer. For further information regarding Agrium&#146;s offer for CF, please visit www.agrium.com. </FONT></P>
