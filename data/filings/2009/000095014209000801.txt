            <p style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 12pt; TEXT-ALIGN: center"><b><font size="2">Advantage Energy Income Fund &ndash; News Release </font></b></p>

            <p style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 12pt; TEXT-ALIGN: center"><b><font size="2">June 15, 2009</font></b></p>

            <p style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 12pt; TEXT-ALIGN: center"><b><font size="2">FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE, NOT FOR DISTRIBUTION TO U.S. NEWS WIRE SERVICES OR FOR DISSEMINATION IN THE U.S.</font></b></p>

            <p style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 12pt; TEXT-ALIGN: center"><b><font size="2">Advantage Announces $252.6 Million in Asset Dispositions and $102 Million Trust Unit Financing </font></b></p>

            <p style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 12pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><font size="2">CALGARY, ALBERTA &ndash; Advantage Energy Income Fund ("</font><b><font size="2">Advantage</font></b><font size="2">" or the "</font><b><font size="2">Fund</font></b><font size="2">") (AVN.UN &ndash; TSX, AAV &ndash; NYSE) announced today that it has signed two purchase and sale agreements relating to the disposition of $252.6 million of assets and signed an agreement providing for a bought deal
            financing to raise $102 million ($117.3 million if the over-allotment option is exercised) of equity through the issuance of trust units. The combined gross proceeds of $354.6 million from the strategic asset dispositions and equity raise will significantly improve Advantage's financial flexibility, allowing it to pursue growth opportunities, including the development of its Montney natural gas resource play at Glacier, Alberta. Together, the approximately $348 million in net
            proceeds will initially be used to repay indebtedness under Advantage's credit facility, which may be subsequently redrawn to fund future capital expenditures, and for general corporate purposes.</font></p>

            <p style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 12pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><b><font size="2">Strategic Asset Dispositions</font></b></p>

            <p style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 12pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><font size="2">Advantage is pleased to announce that it has entered into two purchase and sale agreements in respect of the sale of Advantage's assets (the "</font><b><font size="2">Sale Assets</font></b><font size="2">") located in Northeast British Columbia and Northwest Alberta.</font></p>

            <p style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 12pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><i><b><font size="2">Package One Disposition</font></b></i></p>

            <p style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 12pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><font size="2">The disposition price for one package (the "</font><b><font size="2">Package One Assets</font></b><font size="2">") of the Sale Assets is $176 million, subject to customary adjustments. The Package One Assets are currently producing approximately 5,900 boe/d with an 82% natural gas weighting and an estimated proved plus probable reserve life index of 8.7 years. Proved plus probable reserves of the
            Package One Assets were estimated by Sproule Associates Limited to be 18.8 MMboe as of March&nbsp;31, 2009. The closing of the sale of the Package One Assets is scheduled for July 24, 2009, with an April&nbsp;1, 2009 effective date.</font></p>

            <p style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 12pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><i><b><font size="2">Package Two Disposition</font></b></i></p>

            <p style="MARGIN-TOP: 0pt; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 12pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><font size="2">The disposition price for the second package (the "</font><b><font size="2">Package Two Assets</font></b><font size="2">") of the Sale Assets is $76.6 million, subject to customary adjustments. The Package Two Assets are currently producing approximately 2,200 boe/d with a 56% natural gas weighting and an estimated proved plus probable reserve life index of 9.8 years. Proved plus probable reserves of
            the Package Two Assets were estimated by Sproule Associates Limited to be 8.5 MMboe as of March&nbsp;31, 2009. The closing of the sale of the Package Two Assets is scheduled for July&nbsp;15, 2009, with an April&nbsp;1, 2009 effective date.</font></p>
