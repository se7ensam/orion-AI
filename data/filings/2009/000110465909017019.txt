<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">INVESTMENT IN AuEx VENTURES INC.</font></b></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Toronto
(March&nbsp;11, 2009) &#151; Agnico-Eagle Mines Limited</font></b><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;"> (&#147;Agnico&#148; or
the &#147;Company&#148;) today reported that it had acquired 1,000,000 units (&#147;Units&#148;) of
AuEx Ventures Inc. (&#147;AuEx&#148;) by way of a private placement at a price of $2.15
per Unit for a total consideration of $2,150,000 in cash. &#160;Each Unit consisted of one common share (&#147;Common
Shares&#148;), resulting in the acquisition of a total of 1,000,000 Common Shares of
AuEx by the Company, and one-half of one share purchase warrant (&#147;Warrants&#148;),
being 500,000 Warrants, each full Warrant entitling the holder to acquire one
Common Share at a price of $2.75 per share for the first twelve months
following the closing date and a price of $3.25 per share for the subsequent twelve
months, subject to certain accelerated expiry provisions.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Combined with the 2,202,100 common shares of AuEx previously
held, Agnico now holds 3,202,100 common shares and 500,000 share purchase
warrants representing an ownership position in AuEx of 12.4% on a partially
diluted basis.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The
Common Shares and Warrants were acquired for investment purposes. &#160;Agnico may acquire additional common shares or
warrants of AuEx or dispose of some or all of the common shares or warrants of
AuEx currently held.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Agnico
purchased the Common Shares and Warrants pursuant to the &#147;accredited investor&#148;
prospectus exemption provided in National Instrument 45-106.</font></p>
