FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">AEGON has agreed to acquire Banca Transilvania&#146;s 50% shareholding in BT AEGON, the two companies&#146; jointly-owned Romanian pension business. AEGON will pay approximately EUR 11&nbsp;million for the stake,
which will give AEGON full control of the pension business. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">AEGON and Banca Transilvania (BT) will remain partners. As part of the transaction, the two

pension and life insurance markets in the coming years. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">BT has decided to sell its stake to AEGON in order to focus on its core banking activities. BT
remains committed to supporting the development of the small and medium-sized companies in Romania and its more than 1&nbsp;million retail clients, using its broad network of branches as an effective distribution platform. </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">AEGON already has businesses in six countries across Central&nbsp;&amp; Eastern Europe &#150; Hungary, Poland, the Czech Republic, Slovakia, Turkey and Romania. This
latest agreement will further strengthen AEGON&#146;s position in both Romania and the region as a whole. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The transaction is expected to close in the
second quarter of 2009, subject to prior regulatory approval. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="font-size:12px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px">&nbsp;</P> <P STYLE="line-height:1px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:2px;border-bottom:1pt solid #000000">&nbsp;</P>
