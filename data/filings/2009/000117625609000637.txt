<P style="margin:0px; font-size:12pt" align=center><B>ALBERTA STAR ANNOUNCES GRANT OF STOCK OPTIONS</B></P>
<P style="margin:0px; padding-bottom:4px; border-bottom:2px solid #000000" align=center><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="line-height:14pt; margin:0px; font-size:12pt" align=justify>Alberta Star Development Corp. (the &#147;Company&#148;) listed on the TSX Venture Exchange (ASX), the OTCBB (ASXSF) and the Frankfurt Exchange (QLD), announces that on June 26, 2009 the Board of Directors granted an aggregate of 3,950,000 options of which 3,200,000 options are granted to officers, directors and an Investor Relations Consultant of the Company. &nbsp;Each option represents the right to purchase one (1) common share of the Company at an exercise price of $0.20 per share for a period of five (5) years. The grant of options is subject to TSX Venture Exchange approval. </P>
