<p style=' margin-bottom:3pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:center;'><u><b><font face="Arial" size=4>Abbey profits up over 30%  </font></b></u><font face="Arial" size=4>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:3pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:3pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:justify;'><u><b><font face="Arial" size=2>London, 28 October 2009</font></b></u></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:3pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=1>This statement provides a summary of the unaudited business and financial trends for the nine months to 30 September 2009. Unless otherwise stated, reference to the bank and other general statements refer to the trading<sup>1 </sup>results and business flow analysis of the combined businesses of both Abbey (including Bradford &amp; Bingley<sup>2</sup> (B&amp;B)) and Alliance &amp; Leicester (A&amp;L) (transferred to Abbey in January 2009) compared to the trading results of Abbey (which includes the pre-acquisition trading results of A&amp;L) for the same period in 2008. </font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:3pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=1>The third quarter 2009 results of Santander are also released today and can be found on www.santander.com. The results of Abbey, on a Group basis, are included within Santander&#146;s financial statements.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:3pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:justify;'><u><b><font face="Arial" size=2>Key Highlights</font></b></u></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:3pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:justify;'>&nbsp;</p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:3pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=2><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Continued strong financial performance in tough economic environment:</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:3pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=2>o</font><font size=4></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Statutory profit before tax up over 30% </font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:3pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=2>o</font><font size=4></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Strong revenue growth up over 20%</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:3pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=2>o</font><font size=4></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Cost:income ratio improved to c.41% (Q3&#146;08 ytd: c.50%) and now &#145;best in class&#146; compared to our UK peers<sup>3</sup></font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:3pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=2>o</font><font size=4></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Loan to deposit funding ratio improved to 128% (June 2008: 169%)</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>


<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=4><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Consistent progress in broadening the product range and gaining market share as the bank moves closer towards achieving its strategic goal of becoming a full service commercial bank.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=2>o</font><font size=4></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Continuing to support the UK economy with mortgage gross lending market share up to 20.5% in the third quarter, well above stock share of 13.4%, and lending growth in SMEs<sup>4</sup> up c.18% on the same point last year</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=2>o</font><font size=4></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>817,000 new bank accounts opened in first nine months contributing to an increase of 1% in estimated stock share since the year end, and on track to open 1 million new bank accounts in 2009, with stock of accounts forecast to exceed 8 million</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=2>o</font><font size=4></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Net deposit flows of &pound;11.3bn with healthy contributions across all businesses (Retail, Corporate and Private Banking). Market share of retail flows estimated at c.12.8%, increasing stock market share to 10.3%</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=2>o</font><font size=4></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Investment sales up 32% on last year compared to a wider market decline of c.20%, with net balances up over 30% on the same period last year</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=2>o</font><font size=4></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>More &#145;best buy&#146; mentions than any other UK bank</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=2>o</font><font size=4></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Integration of B&amp;B systems complete, allowing us to sell mortgages, current accounts and credit cards through B&amp;B branches. On track to complete the migration of A&amp;L in the fourth quarter of 2010</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=2>o</font><font size=4></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Rebrand as Santander on track for January 2010 for Abbey and B&amp;B with A&amp;L being rebranded in the fourth quarter of 2010</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:left;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=4><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Reduction in A&amp;L Treasury portfolio on track, 27% lower than December 2008, and unsecured lending over 24% lower versus the previous year.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:3pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:left;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:3pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:left;'><b><font face="Arial" size=2>Ant&#243;nio Horta-Os&#243;rio, Chief Executive of Abbey, said:</font></b></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>"We have continued the excellent progress from the first half of the year into the third quarter. Our strategy of offering value-for-money products, underpinned by responsible lending and controlled costs, has seen us deliver a significant uplift in revenues and allowed us to continue to support the UK economy with increased lending to homeowners and businesses.&#148;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:3pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:left;'>
<u><b><font face="Arial" size=2>Business Review</font></b></u></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;'><b><font face="Arial" size=2>Overview</font></b></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>We have seen a good performance from all businesses areas in the first nine months of 2009 despite continued tough market conditions.  </font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>Our revenue and profit growth continue to improve and we are significantly ahead of our UK peer group on these measures.  Our growth is balanced against prudent and sensible lending and we continue to manage our costs well.  As a result, our cost to income ratio has been further improved to c.41%, and is now &#145;best in class&#146; when compared to our peers.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;'><b><font face="Arial" size=2>Business Performance</font></b></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:6pt; margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>The combination of the three UK businesses is a powerful one. We now have market shares of c.11% overall across the core retail markets of mortgages, savings and bank accounts. The move to rebrand Abbey and B&amp;B to Santander in early 2010, and A&amp;L in the fourth quarter of 2010, is on track. This will mean customers will be able to transact in over 1,300 Santander branches across the UK by the end of next year. </font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:6pt; margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>We continue to develop the range of services we provide as we move toward our ambition of being a full service commercial bank. Our ability to offer better value for money products has enabled us to achieve more best buy mentions than our competitors and has contributed to a significant increase in new business.  In Q3 we opened 295,000 new bank accounts and are on track to open one million new accounts in 2009. So far in 2009, we have increased investment sales by 32% compared to a wider market decline of c.20%.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>We continue to be a consistent mortgage lender in difficult times, with &pound;5.1bn of net lending so far in 2009 (&pound;3.1bn in Q3&#146;09) and a gross lending market share of 18% year to date (20.5% in Q3&#146;09). </font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>Additionally, we have increased lending to SMEs by around 18% compared to the same point last year, and in total, achieved c.&pound;1.2bn of net lending in corporate segments that fit our risk appetite. </font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:6pt; margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>In the first nine months of the year our lending growth was funded by the growth in deposits, with net inflows of &pound;11.3bn across Retail, Corporate and Private Banking customers, demonstrating that Santander continues to be seen as a &#147;safe and attractive home&#148; for UK savers.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:6pt; margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>We have made good progress in Corporate Banking, where our approach has been to identify those segments that we would like to develop and grow, mainly the SME sector and by leveraging relationships through our 20 regional corporate centres. </font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:6pt; margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>Global Banking &amp; Markets took advantage of favourable market conditions in interest rate and equity markets and this, added to its client-focused franchise, saw it deliver a very strong performance in the first nine months of the year. </font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;'><b><font face="Arial" size=2>Integration</font></b></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:6pt; margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>The integration of the three businesses is on track. B&amp;B is now fully integrated onto the Partenon IT platform, which has allowed us to expand the range of products we offer through B&amp;B branches to include current accounts, credit cards and mortgages.  We have also accelerated the integration of our teams, with the transfer of staff between B&amp;B and Abbey branches now in operation.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:6pt; margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>We expect to complete the transfer of A&amp;L&#146;s branches and customers onto Partenon in the fourth quarter of 2010.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:6pt; margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>We are on track to deliver the targeted &pound;180m of cost savings by 2011, with work well underway to remove duplicated back office and support functions across the business.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><b><font face="Arial" size=2>Rebrand</font></b></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:6pt; margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>In May we announced our intention to rebrand our UK business as Santander in 2010. We have made significant progress, having already rebranded the Unsecured Personal Loan, Corporate Banking and Cards businesses.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>Abbey and B&amp;B branches will be the first to be renamed in early 2010 with A&amp;L to follow in the fourth quarter of 2010, once the move to Partenon is completed. This will deliver significant benefits for customers, enabling them to transact in 1,000 branches from early next year, rising to 1,300 by the end of 2010 with the addition of A&amp;L.  </font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:6pt;text-align:justify;'><b><font face="Arial" size=2>Looking ahead to 2010</font></b></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:left;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>We have made tremendous progress transforming our UK business.&nbsp; In five years we have:</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=4><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Introduced Santander&#146;s proprietary IT system, Partenon, in the largest banking IT programme seen in the UK </font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=4><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Improved the profitability and revenue generation of the bank so today we are the most profitable personal financial services provider in the UK</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=4><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Reduced the cost to income ratio from c.70% to c.41%, now well below the sector average of c.56% </font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=4><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Used this competitive cost advantage to rejuvenate our product range to deliver more competitive value-for-money products than our peer group </font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=4><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Introduced and maintained a measured and prudent approach to lending</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=4><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Raised awareness of the Santander brand from around 20% to over 90% recognition </font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>It is this success that has given us the confidence to move to the Santander name in 2010, and with it, deliver the next phase of our transformation programme.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>This will see us further improve the range of value-for-money products we offer, with the emphasis on rewarding our existing 25 million customers, and giving them a compelling reason to do more business with us.&nbsp; We are confident this approach will continue to set us apart from our peers and help us shape the future of banking in the UK.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:justify;'><b><font face="Arial" size=2>Trading income</font></b></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>Trading income up over 20%, with all business divisions showing growth.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>Net interest income was significantly ahead compared to last year reflecting:</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=4><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>robust balance sheet growth in Retail Banking, including 13% growth in bank account liability, and strong margin management</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=4><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>impact of strong competition for deposits and historically low interest rates have been mitigated by a balanced mix of products and proactive hedging strategies, in addition to the benefits of acquiring the B&amp;B deposits</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=4><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>improvements in mortgage margins, both in terms of new business and retention on standard variable rate and other longer term offers</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=4><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>good progress in Corporate Banking with strong growth on both sides of the balance sheet, and with deposit balances almost trebling in size</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=4><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>Private Banking results were also ahead of last year as customer deposits continue to grow strongly, in particular through the Cater Allen business</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>Non-interest income was slightly down on last year, impacted by:</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=4><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>lower Retail fees from current accounts, a reduction in the volume of mortgage redemptions and lower unsecured lending volumes. This decline was partly offset by a strong performance in investment related income</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font size=4><b>&#149;</b></font><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><font face="Arial" size=2>a strong uplift by Global Banking &amp; Markets, in particular in the short-term markets and rates businesses </font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:justify;'><b><font face="Arial" size=2>Trading expenses</font></b></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:2pt; margin-top:12pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>Trading expenses increased c.2% largely due to the inclusion of the B&amp;B related costs following the acquisition last year. Excluding B&amp;B, costs were down by c. 4% with A&amp;L cost reduction of over 14%. Work continues to remove duplicated back office and support functions across the business.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:3pt;text-align:justify;'><b><font face="Arial" size=2>Trading provisions</font></b></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;'><font face="Arial" size=2>The provisions charge has continued to increase, primarily due to the deteriorating market conditions. A cautious position has been maintained to reflect the lagged unemployment effect, but not withstanding this, the performance is still favourable compared to expectations. The conservative level of coverage has been maintained across the portfolio which, taken together with the predominantly high quality prime residential portfolio, keeps Abbey in a strong position. Further insulation is provided through positive performance on collection activities. These factors together with low interest rates are helping to support stable arrears with the level of properties in possession staying significantly below the industry benchmark, based on latest market data. Early arrears also continue to perform better than expected.</font></p>
