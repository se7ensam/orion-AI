<P style="margin:0px; padding-bottom:4px; font-size:12pt;" align=center><B>ALBERTA STAR ANNOUNCES THE APPOINTMENT OF INVESTOR RELATIONS CONSULTANTS</B></P>
<P style="margin:0px; padding-bottom:4px; border-bottom:2px solid #000000" align=center><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="line-height:14pt; margin:0px; font-size:12pt" align=justify>Alberta Star Development Corp. (the &#147;Company&#148;) listed on the TSX Venture Exchange (ASX), the OTCBB (ASXSF) is pleased to announce it has retained Progressive IR Consultants Corp. (&#147;Progressive&#148;) as its investor relations and corporate communications service provider. &nbsp;Progressive is a Vancouver based company owned by Kris Kottmeier, an investor relations professional with over 13 years of experience providing investor relations services for public companies. &nbsp;Progressive has been retained for a period of one year, and will be responsible for the dissemination of corporate data packages, broker presentations, broker communications, mining analyst communications, attending trade shows and handling all shareholder enquiries regarding the Company. &nbsp;Progressive will receive $7,500.00 (plus G.S.T) per month in remuneration and be reimbursed for all approved expenses and the Company will grant Progressive stock 
options to acquire 500,000 shares in the capital of the Company at an exercise price of $0.20. &nbsp;The agreement and grant of options is subject to TSX Venture Exchange approval.</P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="line-height:14pt; margin-top:8px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-right:-4.8px; font-size:12pt" align=justify><B><U>ALBERTA STAR DEVELOPMENT CORP.</U></B></P>
<P style="line-height:14pt; margin-top:8px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-right:-4.8px; font-size:12pt" align=justify>Alberta Star is a Canadian mineral exploration company that identifies, acquires, finances advanced stage mineral exploration projects in North America. The Company is committed to creating long term shareholder value through the discovery of base and precious metals.</P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
