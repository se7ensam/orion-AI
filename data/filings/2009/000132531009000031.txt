<A NAME="news"></A><P style="line-height:14pt; margin:0px; padding-bottom:4px; font-size:12pt; border-bottom:1px solid #000000" align=center><B>ALDA RECEIVES APPROVAL OF &#147;T36 DISINFEX&#148; TRADEMARK</B></P>
<P style="margin:0px"><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px">May 26, 2009 - Vancouver, BC - ALDA Pharmaceuticals Corp. (APH:TSX-V) (&#147;the Company&#148; or &#147;ALDA&#148;) has received a Notice of Allowance from the Canadian Intellectual Property Office for the trademark &#147;T<SUP>3</SUP>6 Disinfex&#148;. On filing of an executed Declaration of Use and payment of the prescribed registration fee, the trademark will proceed to registration.</P>
<P style="margin:0px"><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px">Dr. Terrance Owen, President &amp; CEO, comments, &#147;We are pleased that our proposed new trademark for the Company&#146;s consumer versions of its disinfectant products has been approved by the Canadian Intellectual Property Office. The new trademark is another addition to the Company&#146;s portfolio of intellectual property. Management is committed to building the asset base of the Company and trademarks are an important part of its business.&#148;</P>
<P style="margin:0px"><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px">The new trademark will be applied to consumer versions of T<SUP>3</SUP>6<SUP>&#174;</SUP> Disinfectant products, upon receipt of approval by Health Canada, and has been approved for T<SUP>3</SUP>6 Disinfex Disinfectant Cleaner. </P>
<P style="margin:0px"><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px"><B>About ALDA Pharmaceuticals Corp.</B></P>
<P style="margin:0px">ALDA is focused on the development of infection-control therapeutics derived from its patented T<SUP>3</SUP>6<SUP>&#174;</SUP> technology. The Company trades on the TSX Venture Exchange under the symbol APH and on the OTC BB under the symbol APCSF.</P>
