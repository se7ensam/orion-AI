            Attention Business/Financial Editors:
            Advantage Discontinues Distributions

            (TSX: AVN.UN, NYSE: AAV)

            CALGARY, March 20 /CNW/ - Advantage Energy Income Fund ("Advantage") has
discontinued cash distributions with the final cash distribution paid on March
16, 2009 to Unitholders of record as of February 27, 2009. Please refer to
Advantage's press release dated March 18, 2009 for additional details.
