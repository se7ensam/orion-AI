<h2 style="font-style:italic;font-weight:bold;margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;"><b><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></i></b></h2>

<h1 style="color:black;font-weight:bold;margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;"><b><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">ABB third-quarter results to be positively
impacted by change of provisions of about $380 million</font></b></h1>

<h1 style="color:black;font-weight:bold;margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;"><b><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></b></h1>

<p style="color:black;margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">Zurich,
Switzerland, Oct.&nbsp;19, 2009 &#151; ABB will adjust certain provisions and
accruals resulting in an overall reduction of approximately $380 million in the
third quarter of 2009.&#160; As a result of
that adjustment, ABB expects to announce a net income for the third quarter of
approximately $1 billion.</font></p>

<p style="color:black;margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">In December&nbsp;2008, ABB
announced that it had reserved for anticipated charges relating to certain
compliance, tax and restructuring matters.&#160;
Based on developments impacting the third quarter, ABB has made
significant positive and negative changes to some of those provisions as well
as adjustments to other reserves.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">ABB&#146;s provisions as at the end
of the third quarter will be impacted primarily by changes in the provisions
relating to alleged anti-competitive practices, including, but not limited to,
the European Commission&#146;s power transformer decision earlier this month.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">ABB&#146;s third-quarter accruals will also include an
increase in charges relating to the challenges of doing business in Russia,
including the impact of ongoing tax matters.&#160;
As a result, ABB continues reviewing the situation and assessing its
business model in Russia.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">ABB (www.abb.com) is a leader
in power and automation technologies that enable utility and industry customers
to improve performance while lowering environmental impact. The ABB Group of
companies operates in around 100 countries and employs about 120,000 people.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
