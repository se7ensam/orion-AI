<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">News Release


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Ballard Announces 2008 Operating Achievements and 2009 Outlook Conference Call


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">For Immediate Release &#150; January&nbsp;21, 2009


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">VANCOUVER, CANADA &#151; Ballard Power Systems (TSX: BLD; NASDAQ: BLDPD) will hold a conference call to
discuss its 2008 operating achievements and provide an outlook for 2009 on Tuesday, January&nbsp;27,
2009 at 8:00 a.m. PST (11:00&nbsp;a.m. EST).


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Access to the call may be obtained by calling the operator at 604-638-5340 before the scheduled
start time. A playback version of the call will be available for 24 hours after the call at
604-638-9010. The confirmation number to access the playback is 6325#. The simultaneous audio web
cast can be accessed at http://services.choruscall.com/links/ballard090127.html and will be
archived for replay.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">About Ballard
<BR>
Ballard Power Systems (TSX: BLD; NASDAQ: BLDPD) is recognized as a world leader in the design,
development, manufacture and sale of clean energy fuel cell products. Ballard&#146;s mission is to
accelerate fuel cell product adoption. To learn more about what Ballard is doing with Power to
Change the World&#174;, visit www.ballard.com.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">As per NASDAQ rules, for a period of 20 trading days following the effective date of the
arrangement with Superior Plus Income Fund, the company will trade on the NASDAQ Global Market
under the symbol &#147;BLDPD&#148; to notify the market that the company has gone through a substitution
listing event. Beginning on February&nbsp;2, 2009, the company will trade again under the usual symbol
of &#147;BLDP&#148;.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">For further information, or to arrange an interview with a Ballard spokesperson, please contact Amy
Harada Bradley at telephone number 604-412-7913 or on e-mail amy.harada@ballard.com. Ballard, the
Ballard logo and Power to Change the World are registered trademarks of Ballard Power Systems Inc.
