<div align="center"><b><font face="Arial" size=2>ABBEY AND ALLIANCE &amp; LEICESTER MANAGEMENT CHANGES</font></b></div>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:left;line-height: 150%;'><font size=1>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font><b><font face="Arial" size=2> </font></b></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>Abbey National plc (&#147;Abbey&#148;) and Alliance &amp; Leicester plc (&#147;A&amp;L&#148;) have announced the following changes to an Executive Director&#146;s responsibilities. Alison Brittain, Executive Director, has been appointed to take responsibility for A&amp;L Retail Distribution including its 254 branch network and telephone distribution channel. This will be in addition to her current executive responsibilities for Abbey and Bradford &amp; Bingley (&#147;B&amp;B&#148;) Retail Distribution. </font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>The decision accelerates the integration process and follows A&amp;L&#146;s current Director of Retail Distribution Chris Rhodes&#146; decision to leave the business. Chris will remain with A&amp;L until March 2009 in order to ensure a smooth handover. There are no current plans to merge A&amp;L&#146;s Retail Distribution channels with Abbey and B&amp;B branch and telephone distribution network. </font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>The changes will be effective immediately and are subject to FSA approval.</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>
