U.S.$ </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">CALGARY, Alberta&#151;Agrium Inc. (TSX and NYSE: AGU) today announced that its offer to acquire all of the outstanding shares of CF Industries
Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: CF) for $40.00 in cash and one common share of Agrium for each CF share is Agrium&#146;s best and final price absent engagement by CF and demonstration of additional value. </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Based on Agrium&#146;s closing stock price on June&nbsp;2, 2009, the offer has a current value of $89.01 per CF share and represents a premium of&nbsp;60 percent to
CF&#146;s closing price on February&nbsp;24, 2009, the day before Agrium announced its initial proposal, and&nbsp;76 percent to the 30-day volume weighted average price through that date. </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Mike Wilson, Agrium&#146;s President and CEO, said, &#147;Agrium is prepared to execute immediately a fully financed, binding merger agreement but CF stockholders must
send an unambiguous message to CF&#146;s Board by tendering their shares into our offer. If we receive a compelling majority of shares tendered into our offer, we will continue to press CF to engage.&#148; </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;Our current offer provides full value for CF shares and is far superior to any alternative articulated by CF, including remaining independent or paying a premium
for Terra. We note that the majority of long-term stockholders&#151;including GROWMARK, the stockholder most familiar with CF&#151;have recently sold CF shares at prices well below our offer. In fact, 14 of CF&#146;s top 15 stockholders prior to our
bid in February have dramatically reduced their holdings, a clear signal that they disagree with CF&#146;s views on valuation and are voting with their feet.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman"
SIZE="2">&#147;If we fail to receive a compelling majority, we will conclude we do not have sufficient support from CF stockholders and will walk from the transaction.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Agrium has extended the expiration date of the exchange offer until 12:00 midnight, New York City time, on June&nbsp;22, 2009, from June&nbsp;15, 2009. As of midnight, New York City time, on June&nbsp;2, 2009,
approximately 1.49&nbsp;million CF shares had been tendered into the offer. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Agrium&#146;s offer is not subject to a financing condition. Agrium has
sufficient cash resources and committed financing underwritten by Royal Bank of Canada and The Bank of Nova Scotia to fund the cash portion of the offer. </FONT></P>
