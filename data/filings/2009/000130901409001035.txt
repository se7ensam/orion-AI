<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">News Release


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Ballard Announces Supply Agreement with Daimler AG


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>For Immediate Release &#150; December&nbsp;22, 2009</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">VANCOUVER, CANADA&#150; Ballard Power Systems (TSX: BLD; NASDAQ: BLDP) today announced a supply
agreement with Daimler AG for FC<I>velocity</I><sup>TM</sup>-1100 products for Daimler AG&#146;s fuel cell car
and bus programs. The agreement provides for minimum revenue of approximately $24&nbsp;million over
eighteen months from April&nbsp;2010, with roughly equal distribution in 2010 and 2011.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">John Sheridan, Ballard&#146;s President & CEO said, &#147;We are very pleased to be working with Daimler AG,
a clear global leader in fuel cell car and bus programs.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">He continued, &#147;Automotive is one of the most demanding power applications in terms of efficiency,
reliability and safety. As such, this major fuel cell order for the automotive market provides
further testimony of Ballard&#146;s leading fuel cell product capabilities for commercial clean power
applications in backup power, distributed generation and material handling.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>About Ballard Power Systems</B>
<BR>
Ballard Power Systems (TSX: BLD; NASDAQ: BLDP) is a clean energy growth company with product
leadership in fuel cell power solutions, focused on the material handling and stationary power
markets. To learn more about Ballard, please visit <U>www.ballard.com</U>.
