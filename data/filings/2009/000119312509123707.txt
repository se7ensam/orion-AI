 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="right"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px">

<IMG SRC="g37145ex99_1a.jpg" ALT="LOGO"> </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>AEGON to withdraw from the Group Risk market in the United Kingdom </B></FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">As part of its ongoing portfolio review, and consistent with its strategy to reallocate capital to businesses with higher growth and return prospects, AEGON will withdraw
from the Group Risk market in the United Kingdom, effective today. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Group Risk products provide companies insurance coverage for their employee benefit
programs. Typically, they cover life insurance, critical illness and income protection. The market for these products is generally mature and dominated by a few large providers. AEGON, however, has a sub-scale in-force business with limited
prospects to achieve sufficient economies of scale. AEGON instead will pursue growth opportunities in more profitable business segments such as pensions, individual protection, investments and annuities. </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">AEGON expects the closure of its Group Risk business in the United Kingdom to positively impact future earnings and value of new business. In addition, AEGON expects to
release approximately EUR 55&nbsp;million of capital over the next three years. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="font-size:12px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px">&nbsp;</P> <P
STYLE="line-height:1px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:2px;border-bottom:1pt solid #000000">&nbsp;</P>
