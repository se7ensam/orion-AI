<IMG SRC="g28468txpg3.jpg" ALT="LOGO"> </P> <P STYLE="font-size:12px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px">&nbsp;</P> <P STYLE="line-height:1px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:2px;border-bottom:1pt solid #000000">&nbsp;</P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>AEGON completes sale of Taiwanese life insurance activities </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">AEGON
has completed the sale of its Taiwanese life insurance business to Zhongwei Company&#042;. The entering by the parties into an agreement for the sale was originally announced on April&nbsp;22, 2009. </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The decision to sell the Taiwanese life business is a result of AEGON&#146;s strategic priorities to optimize capital allocation and returns. </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The sale resulted in a total negative earnings impact of EUR 385&nbsp;million in the second quarter of 2009. AEGON expects the sale of its life insurance activities in
Taiwan to positively impact future earnings. The sale has no impact on AEGON&#146;s excess capital. At the same time, the scheduled capital contributions to AEGON Taiwan will no longer be required. As a result, the transaction will have an immediate
positive effect on the Group&#146;s cash flows. In addition, the sale results in a significant decrease of the long-term interest rate exposure for AEGON, which, in turn, substantially lowers required economic capital. </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="line-height:8px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:2px;border-bottom:0.5pt solid #000000;width:10%">&nbsp;</P>
