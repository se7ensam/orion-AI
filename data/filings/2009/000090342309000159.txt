<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>Abbey National plc (&#147;Abbey&#148;) and Alliance &amp; Leicester plc (&#147;A&amp;L&#148;) has announced that Nathan Bostock, Executive Director, will be leaving the company.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>Nathan is presently Chief Financial Officer with Executive responsibility for Products &amp; Marketing, Human Resources, Cards and Insurance.  He will remain with the business until 1 June 2009 in order to ensure a smooth handover.</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>Ant&#243;nio Horta-Os&#243;rio, Chief Executive, said: &#147;I would like to thank Nathan for his significant contribution at Abbey.   He has played an important role in the successful turnaround and transformation of the bank under Santander&#146;s ownership. We wish him well for the future.&#148;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:0pt;text-align:justify;line-height: 150%;'><font face="Arial" size=2>A further statement will be made in due course regarding the allocation of Nathan&#146;s Executive responsibilities.</font></p>
