<P style="line-height:13pt; margin:0px; font-size:11pt" align=justify><B>APRIL 20, 2009 - VANCOUVER, BC - ALDA PHARMACEUTICALS CORP. <P style="margin:0px"><BR></P>

<P style="margin:0px"><BR></P>
<P style="line-height:13pt; margin:0px; font-size:11pt">ALDA's securities filings with the SEC and Canadian Securities Administrators and are available on-line at EDGAR and SEDAR, respectively.</P>
<P style="margin:0px"><BR></P>
<P style="line-height:13pt; margin:0px; font-size:11pt"><B>About ALDA Pharmaceuticals Corp.</B></P>
<P style="line-height:13pt; margin:0px; font-size:11pt">ALDA is focused on the development of infection-control therapeutics derived from its patented T<SUP>3</SUP>6<SUP>&#174;</SUP> technology. The company trades on the TSX Venture Exchange under the symbol APH.</P>
