<p align="right"><font face="Sans-Serif" size="3"><b><u>FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE</u></b></font></p>
<p align="center"><font face="Sans-Serif" size="3"><b>Aladdin Knowledge Systems Shareholders Approve Merger with<br>Vector Capital Affiliate</b></font></p>
<p align="justify"><font face="Sans-Serif" size="2"><b>TEL AVIV, ISRAEL&#150; February 20, 2009</b> &#150; Aladdin Knowledge Systems Ltd. (&#147;Aladdin&#148; or the &#147;Company&#148;) (NASDAQ: ALDN) today announced that its shareholders approved the definitive
 merger agreement, providing for the acquisition of the Company by a Vector Capital
 affiliate, during a special general meeting of the shareholders held today. The
 shareholders also approved the amendment to the articles of association of the Company
 relating to exculpation, insurance and indemnity of directors and officers and the
 amendment of the indemnification agreements with the Company&#146;s directors as
 well as the renewal of the Company&#146;s existing directors&#146; and officers&#146; insurance and the purchase of a run-off directors&#146; and officers&#146;
 insurance policy.</font></p>
<p align="justify"><font face="Sans-Serif" size="2">Under the terms of the merger agreement,
 Aladdin shareholders will receive $11.50 per share in cash for each ordinary share
 they hold at the closing of the merger plus an amount in cash equal to each shareholder&#146;s pro rata portion of the amount, if any, by which the Company&#146;s Cash
 (as defined in the merger agreement) exceeds $12,000,000. The transaction is expected
 to close at the end of March, subject to the satisfaction of closing conditions
 set forth in the merger agreement, which include: anti-trust approvals in Israel
 and Germany or the expiration of applicable waiting periods; the Company having
 minimum cash and cash equivalents of at least $7,000,000 and minimum working capital
 of at least $13,500,000 at the time of closing; Company&#146;s representations and
 warranties (including not having a material effect) are true and correct at the
 time of closing; and compliance with covenants.</font></p>
<p align="justify"><font face="Sans-Serif" size="2">Following completion of the merger, Aladdin&#146;s ordinary shares will cease to be traded on NASDAQ and the Tel Aviv Stock
 Exchange.</font></p>
