 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="right"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px">

<IMG SRC="g98190ex99_1.jpg" ALT="LOGO"> </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Nominations Supervisory Board and Executive Board AEGON N.V. </B></FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Shareholders of AEGON N.V. will be asked at the upcoming Annual General Meeting of Shareholders (AGM) on April&nbsp;22, 2009 to appoint <B>Mr.&nbsp;Arthur Docters van
Leeuwen</B> as member of the Supervisory Board for a four-year term. As announced on January&nbsp;13, 2009, they will also be asked to appoint <B>Mr.&nbsp;Jan Nooitgedagt</B> as member of the Executive Board for a four-year term to succeed
<B>Mr.&nbsp;Jos Streppel </B>who will retire from the Executive Board of AEGON N.V. at the AGM. Furthermore, <B>Mr.&nbsp;Willem Stevens</B> will retire from the Supervisory Board on reaching the end of his third term at the AGM. </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Mr.&nbsp;Arthur Docters van Leeuwen (Dutch nationality) is Chairman of the Holland Financial Centre. Previously, he was Chairman of the Netherlands Authority for the
Financial Markets (AFM), Chairman of the Board of Procurators General of the Netherlands and Director-General of the General Intelligence and Security Service of the Netherlands. Mr.&nbsp;Docters van Leeuwen became observer to AEGON&#146;s
Supervisory Board in December 2008 following AEGON&#146;s capital transaction with the Dutch State. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Mr.&nbsp;Jan Nooitgedagt (Dutch nationality) has
worked in Europe&#146;s financial services sector for almost 30 years. Educated at the University of Groningen in the Netherlands, he worked for PriceWaterhouseCoopers before joining Ernst&nbsp;&amp; Young in 1980. He became a partner of the firm in
1989. In addition to his audit experience, Mr.&nbsp;Nooitgedagt has played a key role in developing Ernst&nbsp;&amp; Young&#146;s financial services business, as well as the firm&#146;s growing Merger&nbsp;&amp; Acquisitions advisory practice.
Mr.&nbsp;Nooitgedagt has held various positions at Ernst&nbsp;&amp; Young, most recently as Managing Partner for the Netherlands and Belgium. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">As announced
at the AGM 2008, the Supervisory Board will propose that <B>Mr.&nbsp;Dudley Eustace</B> be re-appointed to the Supervisory Board for an additional one-year term. In addition, the Supervisory Board will propose that <B>Mr.&nbsp;Shemaya Levy</B> be
re-appointed for a second four-year term. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">All matters mentioned above are contained in the agenda of the AGM on April&nbsp;22, 2009, which will be
available on AEGON&#146;s corporate website <B><U>www.aegon.com</U></B> by the end of March 2009. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="font-size:12px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px">&nbsp;</P> <P
