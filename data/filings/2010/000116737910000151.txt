<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">Alcon Launches <font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">TobraDex</font><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">&#174;</font><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman"> ST</font> Suspension</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-STYLE: italic; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">New product targets inflammatory eye conditions such as blepharitis</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">HUENENBERG, Switzerland</font> <font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">&#8211; September 22, 2010 &#8211; </font><font style="DISPLAY: inline; TEXT-DECORATION: underline">Alcon</font> (NYSE: <font style="DISPLAY: inline; TEXT-DECORATION: underline">ACL</font>), the world&#8217;s leading eye care company, announced the launch of its new combination anti-inflammatory/anti-infective product, TobraDex<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top">&#174;</font> ST suspension in the United States. TobraDex<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top">&#174;</font> ST (tobramycin/dexmethasone ophthalmic suspension) 0.3/0.05% is indicated for steroid-responsive inflammatory ocular conditions for which a corticosteroid is indicated and wher
e bacterial infection or risk for infection exists.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman"><font id="TAB1" style="MARGIN-LEFT: 36pt"></font>TobraDex<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top">&#174;</font> ST suspension was formulated to enhance bioavailability to targeted tissues. This makes TobraDex<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top">&#174;</font> ST a good treatment choice for conditions such as acute blepharitis and other inflammatory conditions where an infection may also exist.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">&#160;&#8220;This product was specifically designed to increase retention time on the eye, allowing the drug to calm inflammation and fight bacteria effectively,&#8221; said Stuart Raetzman, vice president, Global Marketing and area president for the United States. &#8220;Clinical studies indicated that 10 minutes after dosing, concentrations of TobraDex<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top">&#174;</font> ST remained on the ocular surface at levels high enough to kill 99.9% of ocular bacteria.&#8221;</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">TobraDex<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top">&#174;</font> ST suspension is designed with stable suspension properties that prevent settling, thereby ensuring the desired amount of dexamethasone delivery.&#160;&#160;In addition, TobraDex<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top">&#174;</font> ST is formulated with low viscosity in the bottle allowing for easier dispensing. These two factors contribute to making this an ideal treatment for eye conditions where both infection and inflammation exist, like acute blepharitis or bacterial conjunctivitis.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman"><font id="TAB1" style="MARGIN-LEFT: 36pt"></font>&#8220;This product&#8217;s superior staying power offers an advantage for patients to receive a treatment that provides fast and effective relief from uncomfortable symptoms of inflammatory ocular conditions, such as acute blepharitis,&#8221; said Stephen Lane, M.D., clinical professor at the University of Minnesota. &#8220;This represents an excellent clinical option when treating blepharitis that is quite common.&#8221;</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">About TobraDex</font><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top">&#174;</font><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold"> ST</font> <font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">Suspension</font></font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">TobraDex<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top; FONT-STYLE: italic; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">&#174;</font> ST suspension is a topical antibiotic and corticosteroid combination for steroid-responsive inflammatory ocular conditions where superficial bacterial ocular infection or a risk of infection exists. The most common side effects experienced with the use of TobraDex<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top">&#174;</font> ST include: eye pain, eyelid itching, eyelid swelling, eye redness and possible increases in pressure in the eye leading to glaucoma.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">About Blepharitis</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">Blepharitis is a common disorder that occurs in two forms &#8211; anterior and posterior.&#160;&#160;Blepharitis is an inflammatory condition of the eyelids and may be associated with a low-grade bacterial infection or a generalized skin condition. Common symptoms can include irritation, burning, tearing, foreign body sensation, crusty debris (in the lashes, in the corner of the eyes or on the lids), dryness and red eyelid margins.</font></div>
