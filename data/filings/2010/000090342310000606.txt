</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt">&#160;</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.5; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Santander UK plc ("Santander UK") has announced that Ant&#243;nio Horta-Os&#243;rio, Chief Executive of Santander UK, is to join Lloyds Banking Group plc as Chief Executive.&#160;&#160;Ant&#243;nio Horta-Os&#243;rio will resign as Chief Executive of Santander UK on 31 December 2010. This timing will ensure an orderly transition and handover with his successor.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.5"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.5; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Ant&#243;nio will formally join Lloyds Banking Group plc in principle on 10 January 2011 and will assume responsibilities as Chief Executive Officer on 1 March 2011.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.5"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.5; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Lord Burns, Chairman of Santander UK, said: &#8220;I would like to thank Ant&#243;nio for his significant contribution over the last four and half years as Chief Executive.&#160;&#160;&#160;He has been at the heart of the successful turnaround and transformation of the bank under Santander&#8217;s ownership. He has built a strong management team at Santander UK and consequently the bank is well-positioned for the future. We wish him well in his new role.&#8221;</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.5"><br>
