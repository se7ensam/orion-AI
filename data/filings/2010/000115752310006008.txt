    <p style="text-align: left">
      AIXTRON AG&#160;&#160;/ Release of an announcement according to Article 21,
      Section 1 of the WpHG [the German Securities Trading Act] (share)
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      On October 25, 2010, Capital Group Companies, Inc., Los Angeles, CA, USA
      has informed us according to Article 21, Section 1 of the WpHG that via
      shares its Voting Rights on AIXTRON AG, Herzogenrath, Deutschland, ISIN:
      DE000A0WMPJ6, WKN: A0WMPJ, have exceeded the 3% threshold of the Voting
      Rights on May 19, 2010 and on that day amounted to 3.30% (this
      corresponds to 3324805 Voting Rights).
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      According to Article 22, Section 1, Sentence 1, No. 6 in connection with
      sentence 2 and sentence 3 of the WpHG, 3.30% of the Voting Rights (this
      corresponds to 3324805 Voting Rights) is to be attributed to the company.
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      On October 20, 2010, Capital Group Companies, Inc., Los Angeles, CA, USA
      has informed us according to Article 21, Section 1 of the WpHG that via
      shares its Voting Rights on AIXTRON AG, Herzogenrath, Deutschland, ISIN:
      DE000A0WMPJ6, WKN: A0WMPJ, have fallen below the 3% threshold of the
      Voting Rights on October 15, 2010 and on that day amounted to 2.87%
      (this corresponds to 2896348 Voting Rights).
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      According to Article 22, Section 1, Sentence 1, No. 6 in connection with
      sentence 2 and sentence 3 of the WpHG, 2.87% of the Voting Rights (this
      corresponds to 2896348 Voting Rights) is to be attributed to the company.
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      On October 20, 2010, Capital Group International, Inc., Los Angeles, CA,
      USA has informed us according to Article 21, Section 1 of the WpHG that
      via shares its Voting Rights on AIXTRON AG, Herzogenrath, Deutschland,
      ISIN: DE000A0WMPJ6, WKN: A0WMPJ, have fallen below the 3% threshold of
      the Voting Rights on October 15, 2010 and on that day amounted to 2.87%
      (this corresponds to 2896348 Voting Rights).
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      According to Article 22, Section 1, Sentence 1, No. 6 in connection with
      sentence 2 and sentence 3 of the WpHG, 2.87% of the Voting Rights (this
      corresponds to 2896348 Voting Rights) is to be attributed to the company.
