<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.25"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.25; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">HUENENBERG, Switzerland &#8211; October 4, 2010</font><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">&#8211;</font> Alcon, Inc. (NYSE: <font style="DISPLAY: inline; TEXT-DECORATION: underline">ACL</font>) announced today the appointment of Robert Karsunky as senior vice president and chief financial officer effective November 1, 2010.&#160;&#160;Karsunky comes to Alcon from Novartis AG, where he was most recently chief financial officer in the Consumer Health Division of Novartis.&#160;&#160;Novartis became Alcon&#8217;s majority shareholder on August 25, when it completed the acquisition of 52 percent of the company&#8217;s shares from Nestle SA.&#160;&#160;Novartis now owns 77 percent of Alcon&#8217;s outstanding shares.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.25; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">Karsunky will succeed Richard Croarkin, who has served as Alcon&#8217;s chief financial officer since August 2007.&#160;&#160;Croarkin will remain a member of the executive leadership team until November 30, during which time he will assist in the transition of his responsibilities to the new CFO.&#160;&#160;Upon completion of this orderly transition, Rick will return to a financial position at Nestle.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.25; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">"Rick has made significant contributions to Alcon during the last three years,&#8221; said Kevin Buehler, Alcon&#8217;s president and chief executive officer.&#160;&#160;&#8220;He&#8217;s been a valued member of my executive team and has made significant contributions in the development and execution of corporate strategy, including the business development and cost alignment initiatives.&#8221;</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.25; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">Karsunky joined Novartis in 2006 as chief financial officer in the Consumer Health Division of Novartis.&#160;&#160;In this role he was responsible for the division&#8217;s finance, information technologies, procurement and merger and acquisition activities.&#160;&#160;Prior to joining Novartis, he served for four years as the vice president of finance for the international division of Medtronic, Inc.&#160;&#160;He began his career with Eli Lilly in 1991where he had a variety of increasingly responsible financial positions to become the executive director of finance for Intercontinental and Japan from 2000 to 2002.&#160;&#160;He studied business administration at the University of Cologne and New York University, and obtained a PhD in Economics from the University of Aachen in Germany.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.25; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">"Robert&#8217;s broad experience in finance and leadership roles with three of the world&#8217;s leading health care companies will be a tremendous asset to Alcon as we execute our growth strategies,&#8221; said Buehler.&#160;&#160;&#8220;Furthermore, his knowledge of the Novartis organization will be beneficial in optimizing the opportunities to create value for Alcon through cooperative efforts and arm&#8217;s length agreements with our new majority shareholder.&#8221;</font></div>

Alcon, which has been dedicated to the ophthalmic industry for 6<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">5</font> years, researches, develops, manufactures and markets pharmaceuticals, surgical equipment and devices, contact lens care solutions and other vision care products that treat diseases, disorders and other conditions of the eye.
