    <p style="text-align: left">
      Preliminary Results: AIXTRON AG exceeds 2009 guidance with record
      revenues and profit
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      Aachen, February 3, 2010 - AIXTRON Aktiengesellschaft (ISIN
      DE000A0WMPJ6), a leading global provider of deposition equipment to the
      semiconductor industry, today announced the following preliminary and
      unaudited financial results for the full year ended December 31, 2009.
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      AIXTRON is scheduled to release its final FY/2009 results (including a
      Full year 2010 revenue and EBIT guidance), on Thursday, March 11, 2010.
    </p>
