<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 12pt">CALGARY, Alberta &#151; Agrium Inc. (TSX and NYSE: AGU) announced today that the 2009 Annual
Report, including Management&#146;s Discussion and Analysis and Audited Consolidated Financial
Statements, and the Annual Information Form (AIF), are now available on the company&#146;s website,
www.agrium.com. Agrium has also filed its Form 40-F with the U.S. Securities and Exchange
Commission. The documents are available on the EDGAR section of the U.S. Securities and Exchange
Commission&#146;s website at www.sec.gov (for the Form 40-F), and the Canadian Securities
Administrators&#146; website at www.sedar.com.</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">Shareholders may request a hard copy of the 2009 Annual Report, including audited financial
statements free of charge by contacting Agrium through our web page or in writing to the address
below, or alternatively, through the following website: http://investorcalendar.ar.wilink.com.</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">About Agrium<BR>
Agrium Inc. is a major retail supplier of agricultural products and services in both North and
South America and a leading global producer and marketer of agricultural nutrients and industrial
products. Agrium produces and markets three primary groups of nutrients: nitrogen, phosphate and
potash as well as controlled release fertilizers and micronutrients. Agrium&#146;s strategy is to grow
through incremental expansion of its existing operations and acquisitions as well as the
development, commercialization and marketing of new products and international opportunities.</DIV>
