<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 14.15pt" align="center"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">offshore Nigeria</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 27pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">London, England &#8211; May 25, 2010</font> &#8211; Acergy S.A. (NASDAQ-GS: ACGY; Oslo Stock Exchange: ACY) announced today the award of a contract valued at approximately $120 million, offshore Nigeria in shallow water depths.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 27pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 7.05pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">This contract includes the removal of existing risers and the installation of approximately 15km of new pipelines and associated risers, together with associated diving and hook-up activities. Construction engineering will commence immediately, with offshore installation due to commence in Q1 2011.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 27pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">Bruno Chabas, Acergy&#8217;s Chief Operating Officer, said: &#8220;We are pleased to have been awarded this contract, which builds on our strong local presence and provides an excellent opportunity to leverage our expertise, re-enforcing Acergy&#8217;s strong presence in the upgrade and maintenance of complex conventional field developments in West Africa.&#8221;</font></div>
