 Acergy S.A. Annual Report and Form 20-F  London, England – February 24, 2010 – Acergy S.A. (NASDAQ-GS: ACGY; Oslo Stock Exchange: ACY) today announced the publication of the Annual Report and Financial Statements 2009 and advises
that the Company’s Annual Report on Form 20-F for the year ended November 30, 2009 will be filed with the US Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) later today.  Copies of the Annual Report and Financial Statements 2009 are available on our website:  www.acergy-group.com/public/FinancialReports  Copies of the Annual Report on Form 20-F may be obtained either from the SEC or from our
website:  www.acergy-group.com/public/FinancialReports
