<h2 style="font-style:italic;font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;"><b><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></i></b></h2>

<h1 style="color:black;font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;"><b><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:normal;">ABB offers to increase stake in India subsidiary</font></b></h1>

<h1 style="color:black;font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;"><b><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></b></h1>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Zurich, Switzerland, May&nbsp;17,
2010 - ABB, the leading power and automation technology group, announced today
that it will offer shareholders of ABB Limited, its publicly-listed subsidiary in
India, Rs. 900 per share in order to increase its stake in the company from approximately
52 percent to 75 percent.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The aim of the investment is to facilitate the long-term development of ABB&#146;s
business in India.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The offer represents a premium of approximately 34 percent of Friday&#146;s
closing price and 14 percent over the mandatory floor price required under
Indian regulations. The potential total value of the transaction at the offer
price is approximately Rs. 44 billion ($965 million)</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Subject to regulatory clearance, the offer is expected to begin on July&nbsp;8,
2010 and end on July&nbsp;27, 2010. Payment for the shares is expected to take
place on August&nbsp;10, 2010. The shares are </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">traded on the Bombay Stock Exchange Limited and the
National Stock Exchange of India Limited.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">In 2009, ABB Limited, India, reported revenues of Rs. 63 billion ($1.4
billion) and profit before tax of Rs. 5.3 billion ($117 million). The company
employs approximately 6,200 people.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The open offer will be
managed by HSBC Securities and Capital Markets (India) Private Limited</font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">ABB (www.abb.com) is a leader in power and automation technologies that
enable utility and industry customers to improve performance while lowering
environmental impact. The ABB Group of companies operates in around 100
countries and employs about 117,000 people.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
