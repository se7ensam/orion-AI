<DIV align="center" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt"><B>AirMedia Appoints Qing Xu as Executive President</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt">Beijing, China &#151; June&nbsp;7, 2010 &#151; AirMedia Group Inc. (&#147;AirMedia&#148;) (Nasdaq: AMCN), a leading
operator of out-of-home advertising platforms in China targeting mid-to-high-end consumers, today
announced the appointment of Qing Xu as Executive President of AirMedia, effective June&nbsp;7, 2010.
</DIV>

<DIV align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt">&#147;We are very pleased to have Mr.&nbsp;Xu take up a senior executive role in our Company as he is already
very familiar with our operations and management. He is one of our founders and has been a member
of our board of directors since October&nbsp;2005. Mr.&nbsp;Xu will leverage his longstanding relationships
with state-owned enterprises in China to add further strength to our sales operations. We welcome
his increased involvement in the Company and look forward to continue benefiting from his valuable
experience,&#148; remarked Herman Guo, chairman and chief executive officer of AirMedia.
</DIV>

<DIV align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt">Mr.&nbsp;Xu commented: &#147;I am excited to step in as executive president of AirMedia and have enjoyed
working with AirMedia&#146;s senior management since the Company&#146;s inception. I will use my experience
and expertise to help drive the execution of the Company&#146;s strategy and key projects.&#148;
</DIV>

<DIV align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt"><B>About Qing Xu</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt">Mr.&nbsp;Xu is one of AirMedia&#146; founders and has been a member of AirMedia&#146;s board of directors since
October, 2005. Mr.&nbsp;Xu has also been a member of board of directors of Zhong Yuan Guo Xin Credit
Guarantee Co., Ltd. since 2003. From 1997 to 2005, he co-founded Beijing Sunshine Media Co., Ltd.
with Herman Guo and served as deputy general manager. Mr.&nbsp;Xu also worked in the First Secretariat
of the State Council from 1984 to 1991 and served in the Chinese People&#146;s Liberation Army from 1977
to 1984. Mr.&nbsp;Xu received an associate degree in Business Administration from Beijing Normal
University in 1996.
</DIV>

<DIV align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt"><B>About AirMedia Group Inc.</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt">AirMedia Group Inc. (Nasdaq: AMCN) is a leading operator of out-of-home advertising platforms in
China targeting mid-to-high-end consumers. AirMedia operates the largest digital media network in
China dedicated to air travel advertising. AirMedia operates digital TV screens in 37 major
airports, including 25 out of the 30 largest airports in China. AirMedia also operates digital
frames in 32 major airports, including the 15 largest airports in China. In addition, AirMedia
sells advertisements on the routes operated by eight airlines, including the three largest airlines
in China. In selected major airports, AirMedia also operates traditional media platforms, such as
billboards and light boxes, and other digital media, such as mega LED screens.
</DIV>

<DIV align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt">In addition, AirMedia has obtained exclusive contractual concession rights until the end of 2014 to
develop and operate outdoor advertising platforms at Sinopec&#146;s service stations located throughout
China.
</DIV>

<DIV align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt">For more information about AirMedia, please visit <U>http://www.airmedia.net.cn</U>.
</DIV>
