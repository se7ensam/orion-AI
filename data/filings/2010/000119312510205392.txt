 <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>AEGON APPOINTS GLOBAL HEAD OF SUSTAINABILITY </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">THE HAGUE - AEGON has appointed Executive Vice President Marc van Weede as global Head of Sustainability, reporting to CEO Alex Wynaendts. The
appointment, effective October&nbsp;1, underlines the growing importance of sustainability both to AEGON and its stakeholders. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Mr.&nbsp;Van
Weede, a Dutch national, brings considerable international business and management experience to the new position. Mr.&nbsp;Van Weede has served as the head of AEGON&#146;s Group Business Development department, as well as President of the
company&#146;s joint venture in China. Recently, Mr.&nbsp;Van Weede was responsible for Strategy and AEGON&#146;s Public Policy and Regulatory Office. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;It has become increasingly important to enhance the impact of our sustainability initiatives worldwide, and to ensure that they are responsive to
the larger concerns and interests of our many stakeholders,&#148; said AEGON CEO Alex Wynaendts. &#147;Marc is ideally suited to lead AEGON&#146;s sustainability efforts given his extensive operational knowledge and experience, as well as his
appreciation for AEGON&#146;s broader responsibilities as an international corporate citizen&#148;. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">As part of its long-term strategy, AEGON
has taken a more integrated approach to managing its operations around the world, and the appointment of Mr.&nbsp;Van Weede extends that approach to sustainability. In recent years, AEGON has taken steps to strengthen its sustainability performance,
and Mr.&nbsp;Van Weede&#146;s aim will be to build on recent achievements and further improve AEGON&#146;s record in this area. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">In his new
position, Mr.&nbsp;Van Weede will work closely with AEGON&#146;s country and business units as well as with members of the company&#146;s Executive and Management Boards. </FONT></P>
