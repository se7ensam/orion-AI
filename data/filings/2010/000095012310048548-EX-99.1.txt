    2.
     
    The appointment of KPMG LLP, as independent auditors of Agrium for 2010,     was
approved by a show of hands. Proxies were received representing 106,397,247
(99.27%) votes for and 782,985 (0.73%) votes withheld.



     

    3.
     
    The Amended and Restated Shareholder Rights Plan was approved by a show of hands.
Proxies were received representing 98,611,063 (97.02%) votes for against 3,030,827
(2.98%) votes withheld.



     

    4.
     
    The Advisory Vote on Agrium’s approach to Executive Compensation was approved by a
show of hands. Proxies were received representing 103,899,144 (96.95%) votes for
against 3,270,610 (3.05%) votes withheld.
