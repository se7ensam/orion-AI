Ballard Announces 2009 Results Conference Call


For Immediate Release – February 24, 2010


VANCOUVER, CANADA — Ballard Power Systems (TSX: BLD; NASDAQ: BLDP) will hold a conference call on
Thursday, March 11, 2010 at 8:00 a.m. PST (11:00 a.m. EST). John Sheridan, President & CEO and
Bruce Cousins, CFO will review Ballard’s 2009 operating results.


The live call can be accessed by calling +1-604-638-5340. Alternatively, a live audio webcast can
be accessed through a link on Ballard’s homepage (www.ballard.com). Following the call, the audio
webcast will be archived in the Investor Events & Conference Calls section of Ballard’s website.


About Ballard Power Systems

Ballard Power Systems (TSX: BLD; NASDAQ: BLDP) provides clean energy fuel cell products enabling
optimized power systems for a range of applications. To learn more about Ballard, please visit
www.ballard.com.
