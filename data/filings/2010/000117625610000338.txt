<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify">Alberta Star Development Corp. (the &#147;<B>Company</B>&#148;) (TSXV &#150; ASX) announces that the Company and Kootenay Gold Inc. (&#147;<B>Kootenay</B>&#148;), have terminated their previously announced (see press release dated March 8, 2010) agreement for a joint bid to acquire Sterling Mining Company (&#147;<B>Sterling</B>&#148;) and the Sunshine Mine pursuant to a bankruptcy auction to be held on April 21, 2010. The Company is a &#147;qualified bidder&#148; under the Sterling plan of reorganization.</P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify">Sterling is currently a debtor-in-possession in Chapter 11 Bankruptcy in the District of Idaho, U.S.A. Sterling is engaged in the business of acquiring, exploring, developing and mining mineral properties primarily those containing silver and associated base and precious metals. Sterling operates the Sunshine Silver Mine in Idaho and has exploration projects in Idaho, U.S.A.</P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify">Sterling has filed its Second Amended Disclosure Statement (&#147;<B>Disclosure Statement</B>&#148;) in the bankruptcy proceedings. The Disclosure Statement has been approved by the United States Bankruptcy Court for the District of Idaho, U.S.A. (the &#147;<B>Court</B>&#148;) and contains a Plan of Reorganization (&#147;<B>Plan of Reorganization&#148;) </B>of Sterling. The Plan of Reorganization proposes an auction process for 100% of the issued and outstanding common stock of Sterling entitling the purchaser to all assets of Sterling.</P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify">The key dates for the auction process are as follows:</P>

<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify">The Company is a &#147;qualified bidder&#148; under the Plan of Reorganization and the Company intends, subject to Court and Exchange approval, and being the highest bidder to acquire not less than 100% of the outstanding Sterling shares, and for Sterling to exit the Chapter 11 process with the following assets in place: its interest in the Sunshine Silver Mine, facilities, Sunshine Silver Mine lease, and exploration interest in the Sterling exploration projects in Idaho.</P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify">In addition, the Company upon meeting of the above referenced conditions, including the confirmation of a Plan of Reorganization, will reconstitute the Sterling Board of Directors and make additions to senior management of Sterling.</P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify">There can be no assurance that the proposed transaction will be completed as proposed or at all. The proposed transaction is an &#147;arms length transaction&#148; as defined in Exchange Policy 1.1.</P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify">The Company believes that the proposed acquisition of Sterling represents a significant opportunity for the Company to become a near-term, mid-tier silver producer.</P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><B><U>ALBERTA STAR DEVELOPMENT CORP.</U></B></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify">The Company is a Canadian mineral exploration company that identifies, acquires and finances advanced stage mineral exploration projects in North America. The Company maintains a strong balance sheet, maintains qualified management in exploration. The Company is committed to creating long term shareholder value through the discovery of base and precious metals and by seeking to acquire additional world class, advanced stage exploration and production projects.</P>
