    <p style="text-align: left">
      AIXTRON General Meeting 2010 - Shareholders Approve All Resolutions
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      Aachen, Germany, May 18, 2010 - Shareholders of AIXTRON AG (FWB: AIXA;
      NASDAQ: AIXG), worldwide leading provider of deposition equipment to the
      semiconductor industry, today voted strongly in favor of the resolutions
      presented by the Executive Board and the Supervisory Board.
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      The following resolutions were approved at AIXTRON's 13th Annual General
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      Meeting:
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      &#160;&#160;- Dividend payment of 15 Euro-Cent per share for fiscal year 2009
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      &#160;&#160;- Approval of the activities of the members of the Executive Board and
      of the Supervisory Board during fiscal year 2009
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      &#160;&#160;- Approval of the system for remuneration of the members of the
      Executive&#160;Board
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      &#160;&#160;- Election of the auditors and the Group auditors for fiscal year 2010
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      &#160;&#160;- Authorization to purchase and use own shares and to exclude
      pre-emptive&#160;rights
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      &#160;&#160;- Amendments of the Articles of Association in accordance with the Act
      on&#160;the Implementation of the Shareholders' Rights Directive (ARUG)
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      &#160;&#160;- Conversion of AIXTRON Aktiengesellschaft, Herzogenrath, into a
      European Company (Societas Europaea, SE)
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      All resolutions were accepted by at least 94.6 percent of the votes cast
      at the meeting. About 46 percent of AIXTRON AG's share capital was
      represented at the shareholder meeting held at the Eurogress in Aachen,
      Germany.
    </p>
    <p style="text-align: left">
      The agenda items 8-10 concerning the renewal of authorizations for
      capital measures (creation of new Authorized Capitals and authorization
      to issue bonds with warrants and/or convertible bonds) were not voted on.
    </p>
