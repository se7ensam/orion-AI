      Statement
on Verigy Ltd.'s Announcement Regarding Advantest Corporation

December 6, 2010 U.S. time (December 6, 2010 Japan Time), Verigy Ltd. announced
that Advantest Corporation (the "Company") has made an acquisition proposal to
