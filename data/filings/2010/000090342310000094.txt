    <div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Alliance &amp;
Leicester plc (A&amp;L) intends to transfer its business in to its immediate
parent company, Santander UK plc (Santander) later this year under a scheme
allowed by Part VII of the Financial Services and Markets Act
2000.&#160;&#160;This transfer is subject to FSA support and Court
approval.</font></div>
    <div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

provide benefits for customers and Santander.&#160;&#160;For customers this
includes access to Santander&#8217;s full product range plus use of over 1,300
Santander branches in the UK, four times as many branches as currently available
for A&amp;L customers.</font></div>
    <div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

systems and improving the sales and risk management processes through having a
single view of customers&#8217; dealings with the bank, Santander will also benefit
from the significant synergies that were announced to the market at the time of
the A&amp;L acquisition in 2008.</font></div>
    <div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>
