<P style="line-height:14pt; margin:0px; padding-bottom:4px; font-size:12pt; border-bottom:2px solid #000000" align=center><B>ALDA ENTERS INTO VENDOR AGREEMENT WITH LONDON DRUGS</B></P>
<P style="margin:0px"><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><B>October 5, 2009 - Vancouver, BC - ALDA Pharmaceuticals Corp. (APH:TSX-V, OTCBB:APCSF) (&#147;</B>the Company&#148; or &#147;ALDA&#148;) announces that it has entered into a Vendor Agreement with London Drugs Limited and the first order has been shipped. ALDA&#146;s products will be distributed through 70 stores operated by London Drugs in all four Western provinces. Dr Terrance Owen, President &amp; CEO states, &#147;This agreement with London Drugs is a very important step in achieving distribution of our products to consumers. Their stores draw huge numbers of customers every day and this traffic will give ALDA&#146;s products a tremendous boost. As the flu season and the H1N1 pandemic approach, ALDA and London Drugs will be in the forefront helping to keep people healthy.&#148;</P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><B>About London Drugs (www.londondrugs.com)</B></P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify>London Drugs began its pharmacy business in Vancouver in 1945. Over the years, new departments were added, including cameras, small appliances, consumer electronics, computing equipment and software, pharmaceutical counseling, cosmetics, groceries, insurance and travel services. London Drugs now has 70 stores located in British Columbia, Alberta, Saskatchewan, and Manitoba&nbsp;with more than 6000 staff.</P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><B>About ALDA Pharmaceuticals Corp. (www.aldacor<A NAME="_Hlt115846424"></A>p.com)</B></P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify>ALDA is focused on the development of infection-control therapeutics derived from its patented T<SUP>3</SUP>6<SUP>&#174;</SUP> technology. The company trades on the TSX Venture Exchange under the symbol APH and on the OTCBB under the symbol APCSF.</P>

<P style="line-height:14pt; margin:0px; padding-bottom:4px; font-size:12pt; border-bottom:2px solid #000000" align=center><B>ALDA ENTERS INTO SUPPLIER AGREEMENT WITH ACKLANDS-GRAINGER INC.</B></P>
<P style="margin:0px"><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><B>OCTOBER 8, 2009 - VANCOUVER, BC - ALDA PHARMACEUTICALS CORP. (APH:TSX-V, OTCBB:APCSF) (&#147;</B>the Company&#148; or &#147;ALDA&#148;) announces that it has entered into a Supplier Agreement with Acklands-Grainger Inc.(&#147;AGI&#148;). ALDA&#146;s products will be distributed through AGI&#146;s five distribution centres to its 160 branches across Canada and included in AGI&#146;s full catalogue of products. Dr. Terrance Owen, President &amp; CEO states, &#147;This agreement with AGI is an important step in achieving nation-wide distribution of our products. ALDA&#146;s status as an Official Supplier of the 2010 Winter Games has created a lot of opportunities for the Company, especially with other Official Suppliers, such as AGI. We look forward to forging other partnerships that are equally as important as this one.&#148;</P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><B>About Acklands-Grainger Inc. (www.acklandsgrainger.com)</B></P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify>Acklands-Grainger Inc. is Canada&#146;s largest distributor of industrial, safety and fastener products with a comprehensive catalogue, 160 branches and 5 distribution centres across the country. Acklands-Grainger is the Official Supplier of the Vancouver 2010 Olympic Winter Games, Vancouver 2010 Paralympic Winter Games, the Canadian Olympic Committee, the 2010 Canadian Olympic Team and the 2012 Canadian Olympic Team for industrial safety and material handling supplies. </P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
