<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">News Release


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Ballard Confirms Service Continuity For Fuel Cell Bus Customers Following ISE Corp. Filing


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">For Immediate Release &#150; August&nbsp;10, 2010


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">VANCOUVER, CANADA&#150; Ballard Power Systems (TSX: BLD; NASDAQ: BLDP) confirms, in response to today&#146;s
submission of a US Chapter&nbsp;11 filing by ISE Corporation, that Ballard has plans in-place with
partner companies to ensure service continuity for affected customers, specifically BC Transit and
Transport for London. Ballard works with a number of system integrators on fuel cell bus projects,
in addition to ISE, including BAE Systems, Advanced Public Transportation Systems bv, and
Tuttotrasporti.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">About Ballard Power Systems
<BR>
Ballard Power Systems (TSX: BLD; NASDAQ: BLDP) provides clean energy fuel cell products enabling
optimized power systems for a range of applications. To learn more about Ballard, please visit
www.ballard.com.
