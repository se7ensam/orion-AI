="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top">&#174;</font> platform and is designed to provide cataract patients that have astigmatism a surgical option that delivers quality vision at all distances with increased spectacle independence.</font></div>

The AcrySof<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top">&#174;</font> IQ ReSTOR<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top">&#174;</font> Multifocal Toric IOL brings together the multifocal performance <font style="DIS

bly manage their astigmatism without the need for an additional surgery.&#8221; </font></font></div>
