<DIV align="center" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 18pt"><B>Agria Streamlines Operations Management</B>
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt"><B>Beijing, China &#151; September&nbsp;3, 2010 </B>&#151; Agria Corporation (NYSE: GRO) (the &#147;Company&#148; or &#147;Agria&#148;), a
China-based company with investments in the agriculture sector, today announced the streamlining of
the management of its China operations.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">Since disposing of P3A in July&nbsp;2010, Agria&#146;s business operations in China are focused on creating a
profitable seed business. This business comprises three business units: research and development,
production and marketing. Agria has recruited managers with many years of experience in both
domestic and international seed companies to lead each of these business units. Each of these
business unit leaders reports directly to the CEO. As a result, the position of Chief Operating
Officer has now become redundant. Accordingly, Agria has accepted the resignation of Mr.&nbsp;Zhao Yue
from his position of Chief Operating Officer and currently does not intend to seek a replacement
for the position.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt"><B>About Agria Corporation</B><BR>
Agria Corporation (NYSE: GRO) is a China-based company with investments in the agricultural sector.
For more information about Agria Corporation, please visit www.agriacorp.com.
</DIV>
