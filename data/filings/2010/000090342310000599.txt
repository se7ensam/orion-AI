<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Our efficient cost base gives us a competitive advantage and enables us to offer superior value-for-money propositions to our 25 million customers.&#160; As we progress with this strategy we are increasingly rewarding our existing customers with innovative products.&#160; On this basis, from January 2011, we will make our market-leading Zero Current Account available to 1.5 million of our savings customers.&#160;&#160;&#160;</font></div>
