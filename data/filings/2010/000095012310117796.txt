<DIV align="center" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt"><B>Agria Files Amended Form&nbsp;20-F Solely to Include the<BR>
Separate Financial Statements of PGG Wrightson Limited</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt"><B>Beijing, China &#151; December&nbsp;31, 2010 &#151; </B>Agria Corporation (NYSE: GRO) (the &#147;Company&#148; or &#147;Agria&#148;), a
China-based company with investments in the agriculture sector, today announced the Company has
filed an amended Annual Report on Form 20-F for the fiscal year ended December&nbsp;31, 2009, originally
filed with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (the &#147;SEC&#148;) on June&nbsp;29, 2010 (the &#147;Original
Report&#148;).
</DIV>

<DIV align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt">The amendment is being filed solely to include the separate financial statements of PGG Wrightson
Limited presented in accordance with International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS)&nbsp;for the
fiscal year ended June&nbsp;30, 2010 as required by SEC rules. PGG Wrightson Limited is New Zealand&#146;s largest agricultural
services company in which Agria holds a 19% equity interest.
</DIV>

<DIV align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt">Agria is including in this Amendment No.&nbsp;1 currently-dated certifications by its chief executive
officer and chief financial officer. Other than the foregoing section, no part of the Original
Report is being amended. Accordingly, this Amendment No.&nbsp;1 does not include any unchanged portions
of the Original Report and does not modify or update the disclosure therein in any way other than
as discussed above. As a result, this Amendment No.&nbsp;1 continues to speak as of June&nbsp;29, 2010,
except for the certifications referenced above, which speak as of the filing date of this Amendment
No.&nbsp;1.
</DIV>

<DIV align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt"><B>About Agria Corporation</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt">Agria Corporation (NYSE: GRO) is a China-based company with investments in the agricultural sector.
For more information about Agria Corporation, please visit www.agriacorp.com.
</DIV>
