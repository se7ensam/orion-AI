<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.5; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Further to today&#8217;s earlier announcement confirming the appointment of Ant&#243;nio Horta-Os&#243;rio, as the Chief Executive of Lloyds Banking Group plc, the Board of Directors of Santander UK plc (&#8220;Santander UK&#8221;) have resolved to approve the appointment of Ana Patricia Bot&#237;n as Chief Executive.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.5; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.5; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Ana Patricia Bot&#237;n, until now, Executive Chair of Banesto, will take office as Chief Executive of Santander UK, subject to the receipt of FSA approval.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.5"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.5; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Ana Patricia Bot&#237;n has a long track record in the financial industry. After seven years in JP Morgan, in 1988 she joined Banco Santander, where she has had several roles, actively participating in the Group&#8217;s international expansion. Since 2002, she has been Executive Chair of Banesto. Ana Patricia Bot&#237;n is a member of Banco Santander&#8217;s Board of Directors and its Executive Committee.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.5"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.5; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Lord Burns, Chairman of Santander UK plc, said: &#8220;We are delighted that Ana Patricia will be joining us as Chief Executive. Her appointment demonstrates how important the UK is to Santander and I am sure she will make a significant contribution to meeting the next phase of what has been a very successful entry into the UK market.&#8221;</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 1.5"><br>
