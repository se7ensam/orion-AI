<P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Resolutions of the 2010 Annual General Meeting of Shareholders* </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The decisions made by the shareholders at the 2010 Annual General Meeting of Shareholders (AGM) include the following: </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B><I>Annual accounts </I></B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The AGM adopted
the annual accounts for the financial year 2009 and the members of the Executive Board and Supervisory Board were released from liability for their duties. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B><I>Authorizations Executive Board </I></B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The AGM has authorized the Executive Board to issue common shares, to restrict/exclude pre-emptive rights upon issuing common shares, to issue common
shares under incentive plans and to acquire shares in the company. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B><I>Appointment independent auditor </I></B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Ernst&nbsp;&amp; Young was appointed as the independent auditor for the Annual Accounts 2010. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B><I>Articles of Association </I></B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The AGM
adopted the amendments to the Articles of Association of AEGON N.V. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B><I>New Executive Board Remuneration Policy adopted </I></B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">A new Remuneration Policy for the members of the Executive Board was adopted. The policy will take effect from January&nbsp;1, 2010. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B><I>Amendment to Supervisory Board Remuneration Policy adopted </I></B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The proposal to amend the Remuneration Policy for the members of the Supervisory Board was adopted. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B><I>Reappointment </I></B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Mr.&nbsp;K.J.
Storm was reappointed as a member of the Supervisory Board for a term of four years as of April&nbsp;29, 2010. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Mr.&nbsp;Rob Routs succeeds
Mr.&nbsp;Dudley Eustace Chairman of the Supervisory Board </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">As previously announced, Mr.&nbsp;Dudley Eustace has stepped down as a member of
the Supervisory Board. Mr.&nbsp;Rob Routs succeeds him as Chairman of the Supervisory Board. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Dividend 2009 </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Given the uncertain market environment, AEGON believes that it continues to be prudent to maintain a substantial capital buffer, as reflected in the
strong capital position. Though impairments on the investment portfolio improved during the second half of 2009, they remained at elevated levels, which led to lower cash flows from AEGON&#146;s operating units. Consequently, AEGON decided not to
make a dividend payment on its common shares. AEGON&#146;s dividend policy has not changed. It remains AEGON&#146;s policy to pay adequate and growing dividends to the holders of common shares, depending on the company&#146;s cash flow and capital
position. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="font-size:6px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px">&nbsp;</P>
