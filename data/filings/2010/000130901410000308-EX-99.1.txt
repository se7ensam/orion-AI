Ballard Power Systems Inc.


News Release


Ballard to Present at the Deutsche Bank 2010 Alternative Energy, Utilities & Power Conference


For Immediate Release – May 4, 2010


VANCOUVER, CANADA – Ballard Power Systems (TSX: BLD; NASDAQ: BLDP) today announced that Bruce
Cousins, Chief Financial Officer will present at the Deutsche Bank 2010 Alternative Energy,
Utilities & Power Conference in Washington, D.C. on Tuesday, May 11 at 1:20pm ET.


Bruce Cousins will provide an update on the business and discuss Ballard’s strategy within key fuel
cell growth markets. A link to the live audio webcast will be available at www.ballard.com. In
addition, a playback of the audio webcast will be available for approximately 90 days following the
conference.


About Ballard Power Systems

Ballard Power Systems (TSX: BLD; NASDAQ: BLDP) provides clean energy fuel cell products enabling
optimized power systems for a range of applications. To learn more about Ballard, please visit
www.ballard.com.
