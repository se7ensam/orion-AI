(NASDAQ-GS: ACGY; Oslo Stock Exchange: ACY) (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) announces that in order to consolidate the holding of all treasury shares into one entity the Company has sold 10,313,518 treasury shares previously held by Acergy S.A. to its indirect subsidiary Acergy Investing Ltd. Acergy S.A. has an indirect interest in the 10,609,639 treasury shares held by Acergy Investing Limited representing 5.45% of total issued share capital.&#160;&#160;A further 583,000 shares are held indirectly in an employee benefit trust to support the Acergy 2009 Long-Term Incentive Plan.</font></div>
