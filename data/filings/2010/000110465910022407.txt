<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Zurich, Switzerland, April&nbsp;26, 2010 &#151;
Shareholders of ABB, the leading power and automation technology group, have
approved all proposals submitted by the Board of Directors to the company&#146;s
annual general meeting in Zurich today.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">All eight members of the Board were re-elected
for another annual term. The Board of Directors re-elected Hubertus von
Gr&#252;nberg to the position of Chairman of the Board.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Shareholders voted for a payout of 0.51 Swiss
francs per share for 2009, an increase of 6 percent compared to the prior year,
to be paid in the form of a reduction in the nominal value of the shares. The
Swiss-franc share buyback program announced in 2008 has ended. The shareholders
agreed to a proposal to cancel the 22.675 million shares that were purchased
under the program.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;ABB is stronger today than it was a year ago,
despite the worst recession in many decades,&#148; said Chairman von Gr&#252;nberg. &#147;Quick
and decisive action on costs and aggressive pursuit of opportunities globally
have positioned the company for growth as the world economy recovers.&#148;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">About 1,300 shareholders attended the annual
general meeting and 53 percent of the total share capital was represented. They
