<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">ABB
Executive Committee member Anders Jonsson to retire</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Jonsson
to leave ABB at end of July&nbsp;2010 after 34 years with the company</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Zurich, Switzerland, June&nbsp;28,
2010 &#151; Anders Jonsson, a member of the ABB Executive Committee since 2006, will
retire at the end of July&nbsp;2010 after 34 years with the company.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Jonsson is currently
responsible for monitoring and coordinating ABB&#146;s overall cost reduction and
global footprint programs. As of August&nbsp;1, 2010, these responsibilities
will be assumed by the head of Quality and Operational Excellence who will
report directly to CEO Joe Hogan.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Jonsson joined ABB as
a development engineer in 1976 and has held a variety of roles in the company&#146;s
automation businesses in Sweden, Switzerland and China. Before taking up his
current position, he ran the Robotics business for four years.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;Anders has made a
huge contribution to ABB over the years and under his excellent leadership the
Robotics business has become more competitive and a key contributor to the Discrete
Automation and Motion division,&#148; said Joe Hogan, ABB&#146;s chief executive officer.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">ABB (www.abb.com) is a leader in power and
automation technologies that enable utility and industry customers to improve
performance while lowering environmental impact. The ABB Group of companies
operates in around 100 countries and employs about 117,000 people.</font></p>
